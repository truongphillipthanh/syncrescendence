## Why Everything Looks Like This

You asked why you have to explain things repeatedly. The answer:

1. **Context eviction**: Each thread starts fresh. Memory helps but doesn't replace explicit state.

2. **Assumption drift**: AI assumes "annealment" means "organizing files" when you mean "semantic coherence at token level."

3. **Satisficing**: AI tends toward "good enough" solutions. You want "correct" solutions.

4. **Optimism bias**: AI reports "COMPLETE" on surface criteria, missing deeper requirements.

**The fix**: This packet explicitly states what "done" means, what remains undone, and what the actual requirements are.
