# Solana, Crypto-AI Convergence, and Machine-to-Machine Economics

## Executive Summary
Anatoly Yakovenko (Solana co-founder) presents a vision of crypto-AI convergence where AI agents are the primary users of blockchains. As intelligence becomes cheaper, more markets become viable—"the ants are not aware of the intelligence of the anthill." Solana differentiates from Bitcoin (store of value) and Ethereum (settlement) as an "execution" layer handling 65K TPS today, targeting millions. AI agents need permissionless transaction ability; blockchains provide this. The explosion of economic activity from 24/7 AI agents will dwarf human transactions.

## Key Insights

### Bitcoin/Ethereum/Solana Trifecta
- **Bitcoin**: Store of value (geological timescale)
- **Ethereum**: Settlement (slow, secure)
- **Solana**: Execution (real-time, cheap, composable)

"I simply wasn't interested in settlement or store of value because they're not the kind of engineering problem that I'm interested in solving."

### Proof of History Innovation
Cryptographic functions create a verifiable source of time. Nodes can independently verify time without talking to each other. This enables 400ms block times vs Bitcoin's 10 minutes.

### AI Agents as Primary Blockchain Users
AI agents need permissionless transaction ability—no bank accounts, no KYC. Once AIs start transacting on-chain, economic activity explodes. AIs operate 24/7, monitor thousands of markets, execute strategies humans can't comprehend. "The majority of transactions will be between AIs."

### Cheaper Intelligence = More Markets
As intelligence cost drops, more markets become viable. Prediction markets, decision markets (futarchy), micro-markets for every decision. "The ants are not aware of the intelligence of the anthill"—emergent market intelligence beyond individual comprehension.

### Stablecoins as Killer App
1 billion people have smartphones but no access to dollars/banking. Stablecoin legislation enabling $1-10T in digital dollars over 5 years. "Most users won't know they're using blockchain—they'll think they're using a better payment app."

### Victory State
Global commerce that works: farmer in Kenya paid in stablecoins instantly, developer in Vietnam monetizing globally without bank account, small business in Brazil accessing credit without paperwork. "Financial teleportation—moving value at the speed of light."

### Wealth Redefinition
Panel debate on future wealth:

- Peter: Ability to fulfill purpose (compute + nanotech)
