## Daily Life & Productivity

### 1. The 2-Minute Morning Brief

Instead of scrolling through three different apps every morning, I get one simple message. It's the calmest way to start my day.

**Tell Clawdbot:**
> "Every morning at 8 AM, send me today's weather forecast, my first three calendar events, and the top 3 news headlines from the BBC."

### 2. The 'Never Forget the Milk' Assistant

My wife used to text me reminders that would get lost in my notifications. Now, we both just text Clawdbot. Last week, it saved me from a second trip to the store because it remembered we were out of coffee. It's a simple thing, but it adds up.

**Tell Clawdbot:**
> "Add milk, bread, and coffee to my shopping list." Then, when you're at the store, say: "Show me my shopping list."

### 3. The Automated Meeting Scribe

I used to dread taking notes. Now, I just hit record. Getting a clean summary with action items 5 minutes after a call feels like a superpower.

**Pro Tip:** Integrate with Fathom.ai for additional leverage.

**Tell Clawdbot:**
> "Transcribe this audio file and provide a summary of the main discussion points and action items."

### 4. The 'Where's My Stuff?' Tracker

I order a lot of things online for my business, and this has been a lifesaver. I just forward the confirmation emails and Clawdbot handles the rest. Just the other day I gave Clawdbot all of our USPS and UPS shipping labels, and it was able to give us tracking status in a full, comprehensive report.

**Tell Clawdbot:**
> "Track this package for me and let me know when it's out for delivery."

### 5. The Effortless Daily Journal

I just send quick voice notes throughout the day. It's the only journaling habit that's ever stuck and Clawdbot has Apple reminders natively integrated.

**Tell Clawdbot:**
> "Add this to my journal for today: [your thoughts]."
