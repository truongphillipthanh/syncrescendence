Key difference:
- Agent Teams: you predefine roles, a leader agent delegates and integrates outputs
- Agent Swarm: a goal agent auto-spawns agents, then a synthesizer clusters and reconciles findings (random forest vibe)

Agent Teams vs Swarm vs Deep Research:
- Deep Research: supervisor-driven, narrows and verifies to reduce uncertainty (fears being wrong)
- Swarm: parallel exploration to maximize discovery (fears missing something)
- Teams: structured execution with control over who does what
