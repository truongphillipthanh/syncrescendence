# Κ-22000: Interference Pattern & Synthesis Dynamics (SN)

PASS Canon22000InterferencePatternSynthesis:
    sutra: "Six developmental chains interact via constructive/destructive interference, creating emergent coherence."
    gloss:
        Six independent chains (Intelligence, Information, Insight, Expertise, Knowledge, Wisdom) advance individually at Level: 1-4. They interact systematically through @term Constructive Interference (amplification when chains reach complementary levels, amplification factor ~200%) and @term Destructive Interference (cancellation when chains advance at mismatched levels). Full @term Constructive Interference across all six chains produces exponential amplification and omniscience approximation. The framework explains multi-chain interaction patterns, emergence dynamics, and coherence optimization across all scale:1-4 engagement.
end

        Postcondition: All six chains vibrate at refined frequencies in dynamic balance; @term Peak Coherence emerges.
end

PROC CorrectionProtocolRebalancing:
    sutra: "Four-step rebalancing process restores equilibrium between mismatched chains over 4-8 weeks."
    gloss:
        Precondition: @term Destructive Interference pattern identified via @term Detection Protocol.
        Step 1 Identify Pattern: Which chain over-developed (causing overwhelm)? Which chain under-developed (causing mismatch)?
        Step 2 Choose Strategy: (A) Pause primary chain development (rest Expertise, pause Intelligence), OR (B) Accelerate secondary chain (intensive Knowledge, focus Wisdom), OR (C) Reduce primary application (fewer projects, slower pace). Usually combine two.
        Step 3 Implement 4-8 Weeks: Rebalancing period required for new equilibrium; don't expect immediate results (interference takes time to shift); trust process.
        Step 4 Verify New Pattern: After 4-8 weeks, assess: Does overwhelm reduce? Does primary chain feel sustainable? Does life feel more coherent?
        Postcondition: New equilibrium reached; destructive interference lessening; constructive interference emerging.
end
