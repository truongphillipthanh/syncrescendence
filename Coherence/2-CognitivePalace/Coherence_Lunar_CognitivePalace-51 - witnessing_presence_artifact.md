# Witnessing Presence Artifact
**Spacious Awareness Development Tool**

## **OPERATIONAL SPECIFICATION**

### **Primary Function**
Systematic cultivation of spacious, non-reactive awareness that enhances decision-making and reduces emotional reactivity through expanded perceptual capacity.

### **ASSESSMENT CRITERIA**
```yaml
Spacious_Awareness: "Ability to maintain broad, inclusive attention without narrowing focus"
Observation_Clarity: "Precision in noticing without adding interpretation or judgment"
Non_Reactive_Stability: "Maintaining awareness during emotional or stressful situations"
Meta_Cognitive_Capacity: "Ability to observe thinking processes and awareness itself"
```

  Duration: Brief_moments_of_availability

  Assessment: "Evaluate clarity of observation vs. interpretive addition to direct experience"
