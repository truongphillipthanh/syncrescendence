## **FRAMEWORK ELEMENTS**

### **1. Branching Present Focus**
**Application**: When making plans while maintaining present-moment responsiveness
**Decision Criteria**: Prepare for multiple possibilities while staying grounded in current reality
**Usage Protocol**:
- Maintain awareness of present moment capacities and constraints
- Map multiple possible future scenarios without attachment to specific outcomes
- Balance preparation with responsiveness to emerging opportunities

### **2. Extended Now Awareness**
**Application**: When experiencing time pressure or wanting to optimize temporal experience
**Decision Criteria**: Expand subjective present to include relevant past context and immediate future
**Usage Protocol**:
- Include relevant past experience in present decision-making
- Maintain awareness of immediate future implications of current choices
- Practice present-moment expansion rather than point-instant focus

### **3. Discontinuous Moment Recognition**
**Application**: When managing transitions, making changes, or processing experience
**Decision Criteria**: Recognize discrete moments and conscious choice points rather than continuous flow
**Usage Protocol**:
- Identify decision moments and conscious choice opportunities
- Use transition points for course correction and intentional change
- Practice moment-by-moment awareness and conscious selection

### **4. Local Revision Capability**
**Application**: When reinterpreting past experience or updating life narratives
**Decision Criteria**: Change meaning of past events through present understanding without denying facts
**Usage Protocol**:
- Reinterpret past experience through current wisdom and understanding
- Update life narratives to support present goals and authentic development
- Practice meaning-making that serves growth rather than limitation
