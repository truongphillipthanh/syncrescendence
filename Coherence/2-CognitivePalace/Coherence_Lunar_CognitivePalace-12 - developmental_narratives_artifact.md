def manage_developmental_coherence(growth_narratives, learning_patterns, capability_data):
    # Growth-Action Alignment Assessment

## **DEVELOPMENTAL FRAMEWORK COMPONENTS**

### **1. Growth Trajectory Model**
**Decision Framework**: How does personal development occur and what is my growth path?
**Configuration Options**:
- Linear: Progressive advancement through sequential stages with clear milestones
- Cyclical: Recurring developmental themes with deepening integration over time
- Spiral: Growth that revisits themes at higher levels of sophistication and integration
- Rhizomatic: Non-hierarchical development branching in multiple directions simultaneously

**Operational Implementation**:
- Daily: Apply growth model to learning activity selection and development priority setting
- Weekly: Assess whether current development activities align with chosen trajectory model
- Monthly: Evaluate growth model effectiveness for actual development patterns and satisfaction

### **2. Transformation Process**
**Decision Framework**: How does significant change and development actually occur?
**Configuration Options**:
- Gradual: Slow, steady accumulation of small changes creating eventual transformation
- Breakthrough: Discontinuous leaps and insights creating rapid development acceleration
- Death-Rebirth: Dissolution of old patterns followed by emergence of new capabilities
- Accumulation: Building knowledge and skill through consistent practice and experience

**Operational Implementation**:
- Daily: Apply transformation understanding to challenge response and opportunity recognition
- Weekly: Assess which transformation processes are active in current development
- Monthly: Plan development activities based on effective transformation process recognition

### **3. Learning Architecture**
**Decision Framework**: How does knowledge become capability and wisdom?
**Configuration Options**:
- Accumulation: Learning as information gathering and knowledge base expansion
- Dissipation: Learning as releasing incorrect beliefs and clearing mental obstacles
- Cycling: Learning as repeated exposure and practice creating gradual internalization
- Wisdom: Learning as integration of experience, knowledge, and understanding into judgment

**Operational Implementation**:
- Daily: Apply learning architecture to study methods and skill development approaches
- Weekly: Assess learning effectiveness using chosen architecture and adjust methods accordingly
- Monthly: Evaluate whether learning approach produces desired capability and wisdom development

### **4. Maturation Framework**
**Decision Framework**: What does development maturity look like and how do I progress toward it?
**Configuration Options**:
- Endpoint: Maturation as reaching specific developmental goals and capability levels
- Endless Becoming: Maturation as continuous growth without final achievement state
- Regression Cycles: Maturation including periodic return to earlier developmental stages
- Integration: Maturation as synthesis of multiple developmental lines and capacities

**Operational Implementation**:
- Daily: Use maturation framework to evaluate development priorities and capability goals
- Weekly: Assess progress toward maturation ideals and adjust development focus accordingly
- Monthly: Evaluate maturation framework utility for motivation and development satisfaction

### **5. Mastery Pathway**
**Decision Framework**: How does expertise develop and what path leads to mastery?
**Configuration Options**:
- Apprenticeship: Learning through mentorship, modeling, and guided practice
- Credentialism: Formal education and certification as mastery validation and development path
- Autodidaction: Self-directed learning and independent skill development through practice
- Transmission: Direct knowledge transfer through relationship and energetic connection
- Integrated: Multiple pathways combined for comprehensive mastery development

**Operational Implementation**:
- Daily: Apply mastery pathway to skill development activities and learning relationship choices
- Weekly: Assess which mastery approaches are most effective for current development goals
- Monthly: Plan mastery development strategy based on pathway effectiveness and opportunity availability
