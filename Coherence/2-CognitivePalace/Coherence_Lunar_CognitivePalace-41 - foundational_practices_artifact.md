Systematic approach to maintaining biological, environmental, financial, relational, and identity requirements with maximum efficiency and minimum energy expenditure while supporting higher-level activities.

### **CORE MAINTENANCE DOMAINS**

## **1. BIOLOGICAL MAINTENANCE**

### **Sleep Optimization System**
**Assessment Criteria**: Sleep quality, duration consistency, energy restoration, circadian alignment
**Implementation Protocol**:
- **Sleep Schedule**: Consistent bedtime/wake time with natural rhythm flexibility
- **Environment Setup**: Temperature 65-68Â°F, blackout conditions, noise control
- **Pre-sleep Routine**: Adequate wind-down sequence, screen transition before bed
- **Quality Tracking**: Sleep duration, restoration feeling (1-10), consistency metrics

### **Nutrition Management System**  
**Assessment Criteria**: Energy stability, nutrient adequacy, preparation efficiency, cost optimization
**Implementation Protocol**:
- **Meal Planning**: Weekly menu planning with batch cooking opportunities
- **Nutrient Focus**: Adequate protein, healthy fats, complex carbohydrates, micronutrients
- **Preparation Efficiency**: Batch cooking, meal prep, simple preparation methods
- **Cost Management**: Budget allocation, bulk purchasing, seasonal eating patterns

### **Movement Integration System**
**Assessment Criteria**: Strength maintenance, cardiovascular health, flexibility, energy enhancement
**Implementation Protocol**:
- **Daily Movement**: Adequate activity for health maintenance, strength training as appropriate
- **Functional Fitness**: Activities supporting daily life requirements and capabilities
- **Recovery Integration**: Stretching, mobility work, active recovery methods
- **Enjoyment Factor**: Activities that are sustainable and personally satisfying

### **Health Monitoring System**
**Assessment Criteria**: Preventive care compliance, symptom awareness, intervention timing
**Implementation Protocol**:
- **Regular Checkups**: Annual physical, dental care, vision care, specialized screening
- **Daily Monitoring**: Energy levels, pain/discomfort, mood patterns, physical function
- **Symptom Response**: Clear protocols for addressing health issues promptly
- **Provider Relationships**: Established care team with communication protocols
