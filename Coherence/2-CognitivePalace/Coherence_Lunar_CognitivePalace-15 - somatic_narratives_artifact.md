## **SOMATIC FRAMEWORK COMPONENTS**

### **1. Health Foundation Architecture**
**Decision Framework**: How do I approach physical health maintenance and biological optimization?
**Configuration Options**:
- Preventive: Health maintenance through consistent lifestyle practices and early intervention
- Responsive: Health management based on symptoms and problems as they arise
- Optimization: Health enhancement beyond basic function toward peak physical performance
- Integrated: Health as foundation for consciousness development and authentic expression
- Minimalist: Basic health maintenance with focus on non-physical development priorities

**Operational Implementation**:
- Daily: Apply health architecture to nutrition, movement, rest, and physical care decisions
- Weekly: Assess health foundation effectiveness for energy and authentic embodiment
- Monthly: Evaluate health approach utility for consciousness development and life integration

### **2. Body Relationship Philosophy**
**Decision Framework**: How do I relate to my physical body and embodied experience?
**Configuration Options**:
- Instrumental: Body as tool for achieving goals and experiencing life effectively
- Appreciative: Body as source of pleasure, beauty, and sensory richness
- Transcendent: Body as temporary vessel with focus on non-physical consciousness
- Integrated: Body as essential aspect of consciousness and authentic self-expression
- Neutral: Body as fact requiring basic maintenance without emotional investment

**Operational Implementation**:
- Daily: Apply body relationship to physical comfort, appearance, and embodiment choices
- Weekly: Assess body relationship effectiveness for self-acceptance and authentic expression
- Monthly: Evaluate body philosophy utility for consciousness development and life satisfaction

### **3. Health Identity Integration**
**Decision Framework**: How do health practices express my values and authentic identity?
**Configuration Options**:
- Performance: Health practices focused on capability, strength, and physical achievement
- Wellness: Health practices emphasizing balance, prevention, and holistic wellbeing
- Natural: Health practices aligned with natural processes and minimal intervention
- Scientific: Health practices based on research, measurement, and evidence-based optimization
- Intuitive: Health practices guided by body wisdom and natural impulses

**Operational Implementation**:
- Daily: Align health choices with identity expression and value demonstration
- Weekly: Assess health identity consistency for authentic living and value expression
- Monthly: Evaluate health identity effectiveness for consciousness development and life coherence

### **4. Pain Relationship Framework**
**Decision Framework**: How do I relate to physical discomfort, illness, and body limitations?
**Configuration Options**:
- Avoidance: Pain minimization with focus on comfort and ease prioritization
- Acceptance: Pain as natural life aspect requiring presence and non-resistance
- Information: Pain as body communication providing guidance for health optimization
- Growth: Pain as development opportunity for consciousness expansion and resilience building
- Transcendence: Pain as illusion to be transcended through consciousness practices

**Operational Implementation**:
- Daily: Apply pain framework to discomfort response and illness navigation
- Weekly: Assess pain relationship effectiveness for health response and consciousness development
- Monthly: Evaluate pain approach utility for resilience building and authentic embodiment

**Decision Framework**: How do I approach physical movement, exercise, and body expression?
**Configuration Options**:

- Functional: Movement emphasizing practical capability and daily activity effectiveness
- Expressive: Movement as creative expression, emotional release, and authentic self-manifestation
- Static: Minimal movement emphasis with focus on stillness, meditation, and non-physical development
- Integrated: Movement combining functional capability, creative expression, and consciousness development
