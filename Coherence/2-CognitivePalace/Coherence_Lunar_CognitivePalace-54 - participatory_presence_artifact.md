# Participatory Presence Artifact
**Collective Engagement Development Tool**

## **OPERATIONAL SPECIFICATION**

### **Primary Function**
Systematic cultivation of collective engagement capacity that enhances group effectiveness and reduces social isolation through improved collaborative awareness and ensemble coordination.

### **ASSESSMENT CRITERIA**
```yaml
Collective_Attunement: "Ability to sense and respond to group dynamics and collective needs"
Ensemble_Coordination: "Skill in contributing individual strengths while supporting overall group effectiveness"
Systemic_Awareness: "Recognition of how individual actions affect larger system dynamics"
Collaborative_Intelligence: "Accessing group wisdom that exceeds individual knowledge capacity"
```

### **CULTIVATION PROTOCOLS**

#### **Daily Practice Sequence**
```yaml
Morning_Collective_Sensitivity:
  Duration: Natural_group_sensing_capacity
  Method: "Practice sensing family/household or work team energy and needs before interaction"
  Assessment: "Rate group attunement accuracy, note tendency to project vs. sense actual dynamics"

Midday_Contribution_Assessment:
  Duration: Brief_conscious_participation  
  Method: "During group interaction, consciously balance individual expression with collective support"
  Assessment: "Evaluate contribution vs. self-promotion, group enhancement vs. individual dominance"

Evening_System_Impact_Review:
  Duration: Reflection_period_as_available
  Method: "Review how individual actions throughout day affected larger systems and relationships"
  Assessment: "Identify positive vs. negative system impacts, missed opportunities for collective benefit"
```
