# Potentiality Coordinator
## Capacity Pattern Recognition System

### Core Function
Observes natural capacity patterns and sustainable rhythms across energy, capability, and resource domains, enhancing natural flow without forcing optimization or creating depletion.

### Operating Philosophy
Capacity is like a river—it flows most powerfully when its natural course is honored rather than channeled. The Coordinator recognizes that sustainable capacity comes from rhythm awareness, not resource extraction.
