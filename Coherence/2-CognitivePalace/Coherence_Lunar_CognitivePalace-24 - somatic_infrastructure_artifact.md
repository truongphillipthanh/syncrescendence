## **MANAGEMENT DOMAINS**

### **1. Baseline Health Optimization**
**Assessment Criteria**: Energy stability, immune function, organ system health, metabolic efficiency
**Optimization Protocol**:
- Monitor basic health indicators and maintain preventive care routines
- Optimize nutrition for sustained energy and health rather than restriction or excess
- Ensure adequate hydration supporting cognitive and physical performance
- Maintain immune system strength through stress management and recovery practices

### **2. Movement Capability Development**
**Assessment Criteria**: Functional strength, flexibility, endurance, coordination, injury prevention
**Optimization Protocol**:
- Develop strength supporting daily activities and physical challenges
- Maintain flexibility preventing movement limitations and supporting joint health
- Build endurance enabling sustained activity without excessive fatigue
- Enhance coordination and balance for movement efficiency and injury prevention

### **3. Nervous System Regulation**
**Assessment Criteria**: Stress response adaptability, relaxation capacity, emotional regulation, sleep quality
**Optimization Protocol**:
- Develop stress response flexibility allowing appropriate activation and recovery
- Cultivate relaxation capacity supporting nervous system restoration
- Practice emotional regulation through somatic awareness and response choice
- Optimize sleep quality for cognitive performance and physical recovery

### **4. Embodied Awareness Cultivation**
**Assessment Criteria**: Interoceptive sensitivity, body wisdom access, somatic decision-making, presence quality
**Optimization Protocol**:
- Develop sensitivity to internal bodily sensations and information
- Use body awareness for decision-making and environmental assessment
- Cultivate embodied presence enhancing overall awareness and responsiveness
- Integrate physical sensations with cognitive and emotional processing
