## **EPISTEMIC FRAMEWORK COMPONENTS**

### **1. Evidence Evaluation Standards**
**Decision Framework**: How do I assess the quality and reliability of information and evidence?
**Configuration Options**:
- Empirical: Evidence based on observation, measurement, and repeatable experience
- Authoritative: Evidence based on expert opinion, institutional credibility, and established knowledge
- Logical: Evidence based on reasoning, consistency, and rational analysis
- Pragmatic: Evidence based on practical effectiveness and real-world results
- Triangulated: Evidence validated through multiple independent sources and methods

**Operational Implementation**:
- Daily: Apply evidence standards to information evaluation and decision-making
- Weekly: Assess evidence quality consistency and validation method effectiveness
- Monthly: Evaluate evidence standards utility for knowledge reliability and practical success

### **2. Source Credibility Assessment**
**Decision Framework**: How do I evaluate the trustworthiness and reliability of information sources?
**Configuration Options**:
- Track Record: Source credibility based on historical accuracy and prediction success
- Expertise: Source evaluation based on relevant knowledge, training, and experience
- Independence: Source assessment based on bias potential and conflict of interest analysis
- Transparency: Source credibility based on methodology disclosure and reasoning clarity
- Consensus: Source reliability based on agreement with other credible sources

**Operational Implementation**:
- Daily: Apply credibility assessment to information sources and expert opinions
- Weekly: Evaluate source assessment effectiveness for information quality and reliability
- Monthly: Plan source diversification based on credibility evaluation results

### **3. Uncertainty Tolerance Framework**
**Decision Framework**: How do I relate to incomplete knowledge and uncertain situations?
**Configuration Options**:
- Confident Fallibilism: Strong beliefs held lightly with openness to revision
- Probabilistic: Uncertainty expressed through probability estimates and confidence ranges
- Agnostic: Suspension of judgment when evidence is insufficient for reliable conclusion
- Pragmatic: Uncertainty management based on action requirements and decision context
- Exploratory: Uncertainty as opportunity for investigation and knowledge development

**Operational Implementation**:
- Daily: Apply uncertainty framework to ambiguous situations and incomplete information
- Weekly: Assess uncertainty tolerance effectiveness for decision quality and learning
- Monthly: Evaluate uncertainty approach utility for knowledge development and practical action

### **4. Belief Revision Methodology**
**Decision Framework**: How do I update beliefs when encountering contradictory evidence?
**Configuration Options**:
- Bayesian: Belief updating based on prior probability and new evidence strength
- Threshold: Belief revision when contradictory evidence reaches sufficient accumulation
- Paradigm: Belief system updating through framework replacement when anomalies accumulate
- Continuous: Gradual belief adjustment through ongoing evidence integration
- Conservative: Belief stability with high bar for revision requiring strong contradictory evidence

**Operational Implementation**:
- Daily: Apply revision methodology to new information and contradictory evidence
- Weekly: Assess belief revision effectiveness for knowledge accuracy and adaptability
- Monthly: Plan belief system evolution based on revision methodology effectiveness

### **5. Knowledge Integration Approach**
**Decision Framework**: How do I synthesize information into coherent understanding and practical wisdom?
**Configuration Options**:
- Systematic: Knowledge integration through organized frameworks and structured synthesis
- Narrative: Understanding development through story creation and meaning-making
- Practical: Knowledge integration through application and experience-based learning
- Intuitive: Understanding synthesis through pattern recognition and holistic perception
- Emergent: Knowledge integration through natural understanding development and wisdom emergence

**Operational Implementation**:
- Daily: Apply integration approach to learning activities and understanding development
- Weekly: Assess integration effectiveness for knowledge coherence and practical application
- Monthly: Evaluate integration methodology utility for wisdom development and effective action
