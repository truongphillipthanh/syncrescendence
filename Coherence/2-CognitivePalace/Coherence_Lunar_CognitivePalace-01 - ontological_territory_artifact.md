## **FRAMEWORK ELEMENTS**

### **1. Process-Based Existence**
**Application**: When analyzing what exists or how things persist
**Decision Criteria**: Look for ongoing processes rather than static things
**Usage Protocol**: 
- Identify active processes maintaining patterns
- Trace process continuity over time
- Assess process sustainability and adaptation

### **2. Narrative Identity Continuity**
**Application**: When determining what makes something the same across time
**Decision Criteria**: Coherent story connecting past, present, and future
**Usage Protocol**:
- Construct continuity narratives
- Identify essential vs. changeable characteristics  
- Assess narrative coherence and sustainability

### **3. Functional Interface Boundaries**
**Application**: When determining where one entity ends and another begins
**Decision Criteria**: Focus on relational functions rather than physical edges
**Usage Protocol**:
- Map functional relationships and dependencies
- Identify interface points and interaction patterns
- Assess boundary permeability and adaptation

### **4. Genuine Novelty Recognition**
**Application**: When assessing whether real emergence or creation is possible
**Decision Criteria**: Look for irreducible wholes exceeding component sums
**Usage Protocol**:
- Analyze component-whole relationships
- Identify emergent properties and capacities
- Assess emergence sustainability and development
