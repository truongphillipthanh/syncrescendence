**Assessment Criteria**: Concentration speed, distraction resistance, sustained attention, mental clarity
**Implementation Protocol**:
- **Environmental Setup**: Dedicated work space, distraction elimination, tool accessibility
- **Cognitive Preparation**: Clear session intention, mental state assessment, focus ritual
- **Entry Sequence**: Brief transition routine, attention gathering, work initiation
- **Maintenance Strategies**: Natural focus cycles, break planning, attention monitoring

### **Sustained Concentration Management**
**Assessment Criteria**: Focus duration, quality consistency, fatigue recognition, restoration effectiveness
**Implementation Protocol**:
- **Attention Cycles**: Natural focused blocks based on energy and task complexity
- **Break Integration**: Brief restoration periods, physical movement, mental reset
- **Energy Management**: Peak energy for most demanding tasks, matching task to capacity
- **Distraction Protocols**: Notification management, interruption handling, attention recovery
