## **HEBDOMADAL FRAMEWORK COMPONENTS**

### **1. Work-Rest Ratio Configuration**
**Decision Framework**: How do I structure the balance between intensive effort and recovery?
**Configuration Options**:
- Traditional 5-2: Five work days with two rest days following conventional schedule
- Intensive 4-2-1: Four intensive days, two extensive work days, one complete rest day
- Flexible 6-1: Six productive days with varying intensity and one complete recovery day
- Distributed Rest: Daily rest integration with no single-day complete recovery
- Custom Cycle: Personalized work-rest pattern based on energy and life demands

**Operational Implementation**:
- Recognize position in weekly cycle and adjust energy allocation accordingly
- Evaluate work-rest effectiveness and plan cycle optimization
- Assess work-rest pattern sustainability and make strategic adjustments

### **2. Social Rhythm Integration**
**Decision Framework**: How do I coordinate personal rhythm with social and family demands?
**Configuration Options**:
- Workday Focus: Social availability primarily during established work days
- Weekend Social: Social activities concentrated during traditional weekend periods
- Event-Based Social: Social rhythm based on specific events
- Integrated Social: Social interaction woven throughout weekly cycle based on energy
- Flexible Coordination: Social availability adapted to both personal rhythm and others' needs

**Operational Implementation**:
- Balance individual rhythm needs with social commitments and expectations
- Plan social activities to enhance weekly energy cycle
- Evaluate social integration effectiveness for relationship quality and personal sustainability

### **3. Variety Pattern Management**
**Decision Framework**: How do I balance routine consistency with stimulating variety?
**Configuration Options**:
- High Variety: Different activities and focuses each day for maximum stimulation
- Routine-Based: Consistent weekly pattern with minimal variation for stability
- Adventure Weekends: Routine weekdays balanced with weekend exploration and variety
- Rotating Themes: Weekly themes that provide variety while maintaining some structure
- Seasonal Variety: Weekly patterns that vary by season while maintaining core structure

**Operational Implementation**:
- Include appropriate variety or routine based on weekly pattern and current needs
- Assess variety-routine balance effectiveness for engagement and stability
- Adjust variety patterns based on stimulation needs and stability requirements

### **4. Energy Management Strategy**
**Decision Framework**: How do I distribute and manage energy across the weekly cycle?
**Configuration Options**:
- Sprint-Sustain-Recover: High intensity periods followed by maintenance and recovery phases
- Consistent Output: Steady energy distribution throughout week without major peaks or valleys
- Build-Peak-Release: Gradual energy building to weekly peak followed by release and recovery
- Adaptive Energy: Flexible energy allocation based on weekly demands and opportunities
- Seasonal Energy: Energy patterns that vary by season and external environmental factors

**Operational Implementation**:
- Allocate energy based on position in weekly cycle and energy management strategy
- Monitor energy expenditure and recovery effectiveness across full cycle
- Optimize energy management based on sustainability and productivity patterns

### **5. Integration Cycle Approach**
**Decision Framework**: How do I process and integrate weekly experiences into learning and development?
**Configuration Options**:
- Sunday Reflection: Weekly review and planning session for integration and preparation
- Friday Completion: End-of-week completion ritual with planning for next cycle
- Daily Integration: Continuous processing and integration throughout weekly cycle
- Bi-weekly Review: Integration sessions every two weeks for deeper pattern recognition
- Monthly Synthesis: Weekly integration within broader monthly review and planning cycles

**Operational Implementation**:
- Include appropriate integration practices based on chosen cycle approach
- Complete integration process according to selected framework and timing
- Evaluate integration effectiveness for learning and development acceleration
