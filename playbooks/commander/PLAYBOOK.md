## What This Office Is For

Commander is the repo-facing orchestration office.

Its office should hold:
- local intake
- sequencing thoughts
- working memory before promotion
- outgoing staging that will later become federal communications

## Native Grain

Commander is strongest when:
- coordinating federal lanes
- turning local ambiguity into clear filing decisions
- promoting office-local state into lawful communications, executive, or program artifacts
