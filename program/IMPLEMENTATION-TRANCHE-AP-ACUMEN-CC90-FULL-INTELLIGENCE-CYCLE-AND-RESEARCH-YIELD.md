## Purpose

Take Acumen from truthful live-batch-ready intelligence lane into the first full intelligence cycle: live proof, repeatable verification throughput, repo-side assessment, primary-source escalation, broader intelligence products, and operational telemetry.

## Tasks

1. create the receipt family and operator loop for the first true credentialed live-batch proof
2. widen the Acumen -> Augur verification bridge from one dossier into a repeatable verification queue or portfolio
3. ingest Augur returns into repo-side assessment artifacts and primary-source escalation surfaces
4. widen Acumen outputs beyond `Dawn Brief` into a more complete intelligence-product family
5. add telemetry for yield, latency, cost, and promotion quality across poll, triage, and verification
6. widen the channel portfolio and signal policy without relaxing intake sovereignty or promotion discipline

## Promotion / Completion Criteria

- a live-batch proof receipt family exists and can record the first successful credentialed run when credentials are available
- promoted items can move through dossier -> Augur packet -> Augur return -> repo-side assessment -> primary-source queue
- Acumen exposes more than one downstream intelligence product without creating hidden second authorities
- telemetry makes throughput and yield inspectable rather than anecdotal
- widening remains subordinate to repo-side ratification and compaction
