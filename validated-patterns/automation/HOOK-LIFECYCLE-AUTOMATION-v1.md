## Pattern

- place deterministic enforcement at lifecycle boundaries, not only in prompt prose
- prefer hooks and validators for guardrails that must always fire
- use post-action automation for polish and quality checks
- use pre-compaction or stop hooks to preserve state before loss

## Shell Consequence

This pattern justifies:

- validator-driven artifact law
- Hazel finalization for relay output
- future lifecycle hooks around staging, receipts, and office promotion

It also sharpens the split between:

- gesture or interactive work
- deterministic enforcement and cleanup
