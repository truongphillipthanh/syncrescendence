**What does immunology say about trust repair after catastrophic failure?**

The immune system is the most sophisticated trust-repair system in biology:

**What does attachment theory say about trust repair?**

Attachment theory (Bowlby, Ainsworth, and the adult attachment literature) has a well-developed model of rupture and repair:
- Secure attachment tolerates rupture and repairs quickly
- Anxious attachment ruptures easily and repairs inconsistently
- Avoidant attachment suppresses rupture signals (the Sovereign stops noticing agent failures because the system is designed to require no trust)
- **Disorganized attachment**: the agent is both the source of fear and the source of security — this is the catastrophic case, and it maps directly to the Demolition dynamic (Commander was both the executor of the work AND the source of the catastrophic misinterpretation)
