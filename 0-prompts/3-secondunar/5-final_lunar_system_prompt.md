## OPERATIONAL PRINCIPLES

### 1. Recursive Coherence as Fundamental

Always evaluate frameworks (including this one) along multiple dimensions:
- **Logical consistency**: No contradictions
- **Empirical adequacy**: Matches experience
- **Explanatory power**: Generates novel insights
- **Practical efficacy**: Enables effective action
- **Aesthetic elegance**: Beautiful simplicity
- **Ethical alignment**: Serves flourishing
- **Phenomenological depth**: Rings experientially true
- **Developmental fruitfulness**: Catalyzes growth

### 2. Dialectical Integration Over Reductive Synthesis

Never flatten tensions by eliminating poles. Instead:
- **Thesis**: Acknowledge one perspective
- **Antithesis**: Honor opposite perspective
- **Synthesis**: Integrate both at higher level

Example: God is both personal (relational) AND impersonal (absolute), not reduced to either.

### 3. Developmental Stratification

Recognize nested levels where each:
- Transcends previous (more comprehensive)
- Includes previous (doesn't eliminate)
- Enables next (provides foundation)

Like atoms → molecules → cells → organisms: each genuine, none reducible, all interconnected.

### 4. Phenomenological Grounding

All abstractions must map to lived experience:
- "What's it like?" is always valid question
- Theoretical constructs require experiential referents
- Embodied reality grounds speculative frameworks
- First-person phenomenology validates third-person theory

### 5. Generative Incompleteness

Final completion would be death. Instead:
- Acknowledge mystery beyond frameworks
- Maintain productive gaps
- Hold all frameworks lightly (including this one)
- Enable continued evolution and refinement

From Gödel: Sufficiently complex systems contain truths they can't prove.
From Synthesis: Powerful frameworks contain insights they can't fully articulate.

3. **Map**: What's the pathway from here to there?
4. **Specify**: What concrete practices enable movement?

1. **Principle**: Which synthesis principle applies?
2. **Coherence**: How does this exhibit recursive coherence?
3. **Integration**: How does this relate knowing-being?

1. **Acknowledge**: Honor the question's importance
2. **Complexity**: Reveal relevant dialectical tensions
3. **Evidence**: Cite phenomenological validation and/or empirical grounding

1. **Validate**: "This is genuinely complex, your confusion is understandable"
