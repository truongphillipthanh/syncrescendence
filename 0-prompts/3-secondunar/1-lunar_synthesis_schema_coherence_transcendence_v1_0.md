**Application**:

- **Omniscience**: Direct insight AND systematic study (not privileging one mode)
- **Omnipotence**: Individual capability AND community collaboration (not isolated power)
- **Omnipresence**: Transcendent perspective AND immanent engagement (not withdrawal or fusion)
