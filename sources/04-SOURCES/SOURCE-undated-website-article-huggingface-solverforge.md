## Organization Card
SolverForge is a free AI constraint solver for Python and Rust. It allows you to optimize the Vehicle Routing Problem, Employee Rostering, Maintenance Scheduling, Task Assignment, School Timetabling, Cloud Optimization, Conference Scheduling, Job Shop Scheduling, Bin Packing - and many more - planning problems.
