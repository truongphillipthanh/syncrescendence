### C. The Qualification Mechanism (Critical Filter)

**Per-Video Assessment Criteria**:

1. **Signal Density**: Information per minute (is this worth 15/30/60 minutes?)
2. **Novelty**: New information vs. rehash of known patterns
3. **Synthesis Quality**: Does it connect disparate domains meaningfully?
4. **Source Proximity**: Primary source? Expert synthesis? Commentary on commentary?
5. **Temporal Relevance**: Breaking news? Evergreen wisdom? Speculative futurism?
6. **Actionability**: Does this change how I think/act, or just entertain?

**Quality Tiers**:
- **Tier 1 (Immediate consumption)**: Primary sources, paradigm shifts, expert synthesis, directly actionable
- **Tier 2 (Curated queue)**: High-quality but not urgent, batch for synthesis sessions
- **Tier 3 (Archive/Reference)**: Valuable but low priority, keep for future deep-dives
- **Tier 4 (Prune)**: Redundant, low signal, entertainment masquerading as education

**15-Minute Rule**: Many 15-minute videos could be 2-minute summaries. Qualification mechanism should extract the insight without consuming the full runtime unless depth warrants it.

