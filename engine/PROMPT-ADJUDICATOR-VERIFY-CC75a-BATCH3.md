## Verification Protocol

For each of the 30 entries:

1. **Read the neocorpus entry** in full.
2. **Read EVERY cited source file** in `corpus/<topic>/`. For CANON sources, read from `canon/`.
3. **Verify each claim** against its cited source:
   - **VERIFIED** — claim accurately reflects source
   - **UNSUPPORTED** — claim not found in cited source
   - **DISTORTED** — claim misrepresents or exaggerates
   - **FABRICATED** — no basis in any cited source
   - **CITATION ERROR** — wrong source ID cited
4. **Check for orphan claims** — assertions citing no source.
5. **Check for missing wisdom** — significant insights the entry failed to capture.
