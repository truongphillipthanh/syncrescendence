**Old frame**: The canon is a garden. Growth requires pruning. 5:1 apoptosis ratio = controlled contraction.

Growth is fusion. 5:1 ratio = 5 atoms condense into 1 denser axiom, releasing epistemic binding energy (reduced fragmentation, freed context, resolved contradictions). **Action**: Update retirement_protocol.md to explicitly exempt cognitive instruments. Add category: `class: instrument` (exempt from 2:1), distinct from `class: tool` and `class: platform`.
