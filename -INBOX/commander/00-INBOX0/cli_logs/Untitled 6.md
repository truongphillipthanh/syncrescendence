  Chorus Coalescence: What the 4 Voices Agree On

  All 4 platforms independently identified the same architectural truth: Palantir's ontology
  operates on 3 layers — Semantic (what exists), Kinetic (what can be done), Dynamic (how it
  evolves). Our PROJ-006b substrate currently has robust Semantic coverage but zero Kinetic or
   Dynamic layers.

  The analogy: we built a map with landmarks but no roads and no traffic laws.

  The Key Insight Per Voice

  - ChatGPT: Most actionable — 8 personal ontology objects (Commitment, Boundary, Capacity,
  Risk, Value, Relationship, Project, Identity facet) + 8 action verb categories
  - Claude: Deepest — "epistemological commitment," hermeneutic circle of self-interpretation,
   "the oldest political question wearing new clothes"
  - Gemini: Best naming — XRP (Existential Resource Planning), ISP (Inter-Subjective
  Protocol), ASO (Agentic Swarm Orchestration), RRE (Reality Rendering Engine)
  - Grok: Most visionary — "the ontology ceases to be a corporate map and becomes the
  soul-schema"
