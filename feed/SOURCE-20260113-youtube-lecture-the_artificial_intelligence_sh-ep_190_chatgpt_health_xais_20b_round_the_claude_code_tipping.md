In this episode, we explore the shift in AI capabilities that allows for non-technical leaders to build software and solve complex business problems in minutes.

Mike Kaput and Paul Roetzer analyze the launch of ChatGPT Health and its implications for personal medical data privacy, as well as the "tipping point" our audience is experiencing with Claude Code. From Elon Musk’s $20B Series E for xAI to the new partnership between Boston Dynamics and Google DeepMind, we cover this week’s most important topics in AI.

We discuss:
- ChatGPT Health & Anthropic for Healthcare: How AI is grounding itself in personal medical records to support care.
- The 10x Professional: Use cases harnessing Lovable and Claude Code to automate analysis and development.
- The Trillion-Dollar Race: Breaking down the massive funding rounds for xAI and Anthropic.
- Humanoid Robotics: The integration of Gemini into Boston Dynamics' Atlas.
- The Competitive Landscape: Why Google Gemini is rapidly gaining ground on ChatGPT.
