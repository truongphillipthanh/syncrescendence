This is a unifying layer that coordinates disparate applications into a genuine digital twin, acting as the proverbial "glove" for the human hand in our "hand-in-glove" symbiosis. We will decompose complex workflows into their atomic units (`Area ⊃ System → Process ⊃ Activity → Task`) and re-synthesize them into more potent configurations.

* **The Aesthetic Standard: Goldilocks Precision.** Our models must be complex enough for holistic coverage but elegant enough to be approachable and heuristic. We seek the "just-in-time, ephemeral, context-specific software" that feels inevitable and is obscured by superior design simplicity.
