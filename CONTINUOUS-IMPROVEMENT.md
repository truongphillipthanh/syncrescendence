# Continuous Improvement

Every repeated line of work should tighten the shell.

Directive-close loop:

1. verify receipts
2. check translation quality
3. record anti-patterns
4. propose one improvement
5. compact successful repetition into a playbook, operator, validated pattern, or regulation artifact

Weekly loop:

1. review completed work
2. score repeatability and quality
3. decide whether the line stays manual, should be white-labeled, or should be verticalized
