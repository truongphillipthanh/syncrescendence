Dwarkesh Patel is a podcaster, author, and public intellectual, most known for his deeply researched interviews with influential figures on topics of technology, science, economics, and artificial intelligence (AI). He is the host of the Dwarkesh Podcast, formerly known as The Lunar Society Podcast. Despite his young age, Patel has earned praise for his thoughtful and probing interviews from some of the biggest names in tech. 
Background and education
- Early life and education: Originally from India, Patel's family moved to the United States around his ninth birthday. After his family lived in several different states, he earned a Computer Science degree from the University of Texas at Austin, graduating early in 2021.
- The podcast's origin: While in college during the COVID-19 pandemic, Patel started his podcast from his dorm room out of intellectual curiosity and boredom. He secured his first guest, economist Bryan Caplan, with a cold email. His early guests were primarily academic intellectuals, and the podcast gained recognition within "intellectual Twitter" and progress studies communities. 
The Dwarkesh Podcast
What sets Patel's podcast apart is his meticulous preparation and focus on deep, intellectual inquiry. 
- Deep research: Before each interview, Patel dedicates extensive time to reading and studying his guests' work and fields of expertise. This allows him to ask specific, nuanced, and original questions that go beyond standard talking points.
- Guest list: He has interviewed a wide range of top scientists, founders, and public figures, including Mark Zuckerberg, Marc Andreessen, and AI researchers like Ilya Sutskever.
- Critical acclaim: Tech leaders like Jeff Bezos and Paul Graham have publicly praised Patel's work, which has helped expand his influence and audience. Time Magazine also named him one of the "100 Most Influential People in AI" in 2024.
