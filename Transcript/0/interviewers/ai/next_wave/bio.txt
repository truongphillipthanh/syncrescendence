The Next Wave is a podcast focused on artificial intelligence (AI) and the future of technology, hosted by AI experts and entrepreneurs Matt Wolfe and Nathan Lands. The hosts aim to act as a "personal Chief AI Officer" for listeners, distilling complex AI trends into practical, business-focused insights.

Show format and purpose
The Next Wave is designed to help business owners and professionals navigate the complexities of AI, whether they are just beginning to explore the technology or are seeking a deeper understanding.
* Democratized expertise: The podcast aims to share high-level AI knowledge that is usually reserved for the boardrooms of major corporations, making it accessible to a wider audience.
* Practical application: Rather than focusing only on technical details, the hosts explain how AI can be implemented to foster business growth and adapt to the modern AI era.
* Actionable insights: Listeners can expect to receive trustworthy perspectives on how AI will shape their industry, along with actionable advice.

About the hosts
* Matt Wolfe: An American entrepreneur known for his AI tool curation platform, FutureTools.io, and for being the former co-host of The Hustle & Flowchart Podcast.
* Nathan Lands: An AI expert who, alongside Matt Wolfe, co-hosts the podcast.

Where to listen
You can find The Next Wave on various platforms, including Apple Podcasts, YouTube, and the HubSpot Media Network.
