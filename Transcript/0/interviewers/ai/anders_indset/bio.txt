Anders Indset
Anders Indset is the host of The Quantum Economy Podcast, a business philosopher, and deep-tech investor known for bridging humanity and technology. His work focuses on how technologies like quantum computing, AI, and biotechnology are reshaping the economy and our understanding of human progress.
Background and Expertise
- Business Philosopher: Often called "the business philosopher," Indset is recognized for his unique approach to practical philosophy. He emphasizes that in the current age of exponential technology, understanding and critical thinking are more important for leaders than simply accumulating knowledge.
- Deep-Tech Investor: As the founder and CEO of Njordis Group, a venture capital and advisory firm, he invests in and supports deep-tech companies with the goal of delivering positive progress for humanity.
- Best-Selling Author: Indset is the author of several best-selling books, including The Quantum Economy, The Singularity Paradox, and The Viking Code.
- The Quantum Economy Concept: The podcast is an extension of his book of the same name, where he offers a holistic perspective on a "humane capitalism" that aligns with the principles of natural systems.
The Podcast
- On the podcast, Indset engages in conversations with leading CEOs, founders, scientists, and philosophers who are active in the emerging quantum economy.
- The discussions go beyond the technological hype to explore the deeper societal and economic implications of quantum innovation.
- The show offers accessible, high-level dialogues designed to help listeners reflect on the real-world impact of technological shifts.
