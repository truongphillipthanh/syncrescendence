Intelligent Machines is a weekly podcast from the TWiT.tv network that explores the AI revolution, covering its promise, perils, and impact on modern life. The program, formerly known as This Week in Google (TWiG), was renamed in January 2025 to reflect its primary focus on artificial intelligence.

Hosts
- Leo Laporte: The founder of the TWiT podcast network and a veteran media personality.

The program, formerly known as This Week in Google (TWiG), was renamed in January 2025 to reflect its primary focus on artificial intelligence.

Hosts
- Leo Laporte: The founder of the TWiT podcast network and a veteran media personality.
- Jeff Jarvis: The director of the Tow-Knight Center for Entrepreneurial Journalism at CUNY's Craig Newmark Graduate School of Journalism.
- Paris Martineau: An investigative journalist at The Information.

What the podcast covers
Each episode features the hosts interviewing AI pioneers, inventors, and innovators to cut through the hype and provide a deeper understanding of the field. The hosts also engage in lively discussion and debate on a wide range of AI-related topics, including:
- The effects of AI on big tech, regulations, ethics, and social impacts.
- The latest technology news and obscure, but relevant, topics.
- How AI is disrupting everything from smart devices and self-driving cars to the future of work and privacy.

Target audience
The podcast is aimed at both tech experts and general listeners who are interested in staying informed about AI and its future implications.

Where to find it
- Website: twit.tv/shows/intelligent-machines
- Platforms: Amazon Music, Apple Podcasts, Pocket Casts, Podbean, and YouTube.
