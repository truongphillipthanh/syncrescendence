Matthew Berman is an AI enthusiast and founder of Forward Future. He is a San Francisco-based engineer and entrepreneur who is an active voice in the AI community, known for making complex concepts accessible to a wide audience. 
Key career highlights
- Startup founder: Berman has a proven track record as an entrepreneur.
- Sonar Technologies: In 2014, he founded Sonar Technologies, a company that grew to 25 employees and was acquired in 2019.
- Teamly.com: He co-founded Teamly.com, a platform backed by 500 Startups.
- Forward Future: In 2023, he established Forward Future, a company focused on AI education, consulting, development, and investment.
- AI influencer: He has a strong presence online, particularly on YouTube, where his channel has hundreds of thousands of subscribers. He shares his knowledge on AI, machine learning, and emerging tech trends through educational content.
- Educational mission: Through his ventures like Forward Future, Berman aims to make AI more understandable and useful for individuals and organizations. He also publishes a popular AI-focused newsletter.
- Podcast guest and writer: He frequently appears on podcasts, such as Dylan Curious's AI Pod, and is published in media outlets. 
Expertise and vision
- Explaining AI: Berman is known for his ability to demystify complex AI phenomena, such as "hallucinations" in large language models, by explaining them as a function of pattern momentum rather than a bug.
- Human-centric AI: He advocates for a future where AI empowers human productivity and creativity, rather than replacing it.
- Predicting AI trends: Berman offers informed insights on the future trajectory of AI, including the possibilities of a "hard" versus "smooth" takeoff scenario for artificial superintelligence.
