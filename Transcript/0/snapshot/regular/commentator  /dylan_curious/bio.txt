Dylan Curious (real name Dylan Jorgensen) is an AI enthusiast, futurist, and tech educator known for his insightful YouTube videos and for co-hosting the podcast AI Pod with Wes Roth. He is also an entrepreneur with a background in software startups and organizational science.
Career and professional background
- YouTube content creator: In February 2022, Curious launched his YouTube channel with the goal of making AI less confusing for the average person. He explores real-world applications of AI, the human side of technology, and the intersection of technology, history, and future possibilities. His approach is known for being grounded in facts and for avoiding personal biases.
- Podcast host: He and Wes Roth host the AI Pod podcast, where they interview top experts, researchers, and developers in AI, robotics, and biotech.
- Startup founder: Early in his career, he served as the founder and Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of the software startup Ticket Cake, which was eventually funded by Zappos CEO Tony Hsieh.
- Organizational sciences and data analytics: After his startup, Curious worked at Zappos in organizational sciences, where he analyzed company culture and team dynamics using data science and graph algorithms. He credits his experiences at Zappos and with Tony Hsieh for teaching him about building community and putting people first.
- Other ventures: He has also held roles as a Social Systems Analyst for the Downtown Project in Las Vegas and as an AI Educator at the Mnemonic Academy, where he developed programming courses for Udemy.
Approach and philosophy
- Making AI accessible: Curious focuses on making complex concepts accessible without sacrificing depth. His videos and interviews are designed to be a "discovery journey" for the audience.
- Human-centered perspective: Unlike many tech influencers, Curious emphasizes the human and philosophical implications of AI.
- Humility and authenticity: His content is notable for his humility and authentic approach. He prefers to put the focus on the subject matter and his guests rather than his own accomplishments.
- Creative use of AI: He often uses creative elements to bring his topics to life, such as integrating an AI language model with a robotic transformer named Grimlock for his videos.
