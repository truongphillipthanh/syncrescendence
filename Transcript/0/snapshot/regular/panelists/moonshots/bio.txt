Dr. Peter H. Diamandis, recognized by Fortune as one of the "World's 50 Greatest Leaders," is an engineer, physician, and serial entrepreneur. On the Moonshots with Peter Diamandis podcast, he convenes fellow experts to explore exponential technologies and inspire listeners to pursue ambitious, world-changing goals. 
Diamandis is joined by a rotating panel of experts, including:
- Dave Blundin: Founder and General Partner of Link Ventures.
- Salim Ismail: Founder of OpenExO.
- Dr. Alexander Wissner-Gross: A computer scientist, physicist, and investor.
- Emad Mostaque: Former CEO of Stability AI and Founder of Intelligent Internet. 
Bio for Peter H. Diamandis
- Pioneer in incentive competitions: Diamandis founded the XPRIZE Foundation, which creates large-scale, incentivized competitions to inspire breakthroughs in areas like space and health.
- Expert on exponential technology: He is the co-founder and executive chairman of Singularity University, an organization focused on understanding and applying rapidly advancing technologies to solve global challenges.
- Bestselling author: Diamandis has co-authored several New York Times bestsellers, including Abundance, Bold, and The Future is Faster Than You Think.
- Inspiring changemakers: Through his work, including the podcast, he aims to foster an "abundance mindset" and help leaders tackle major global issues.
- Diverse expertise: With a background from Harvard and MIT, Diamandis leverages his multidisciplinary knowledge to find innovative solutions in fields like longevity, space, and AI. 
Panelist bios
- Dave Blundin: As a venture capitalist and founder of Link Ventures, Blundin brings deep investment and market insight to the panel. His expertise helps ground discussions on exponential technology in real-world business applications.
- Salim Ismail: The founder of OpenExO and a technology strategist, Ismail's focus is on how organizations can leverage exponential technologies to transform and scale. His perspectives on business disruption are a cornerstone of the panel's conversations.
- Dr. Alexander Wissner-Gross: As a computer scientist and investor, Dr. Wissner-Gross provides a rigorous, technical perspective, particularly in areas concerning AI, complex systems, and the foundational science behind emerging technologies.
- Emad Mostaque: A mathematician and serial entrepreneur, Mostaque is known for founding Stability AI and leading the development of systems like Stable Diffusion. His focus is on decentralizing AI to promote transparency and innovation. After leaving Stability AI, he founded Intelligent Internet, focusing on the potential of decentralization in the field.
