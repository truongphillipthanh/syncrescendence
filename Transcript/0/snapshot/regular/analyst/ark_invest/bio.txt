The ARK Invest podcast is not a single show but a collection of podcasts and video series produced by ARK Investment Management, focusing on disruptive innovation. While ARK's Chief Executive Officer and Chief Investment Officer, Cathie Wood, is a frequent and prominent guest, a rotating cast of ARK's analysts and specialists hosts the various series. 

Overview of ARK Invest's podcasts
- Purpose: To offer intellectual discussion and research-driven insights into disruptive innovation, including AI, robotics, fintech, genomics, and energy storage.
- Content: Episodes feature ARK analysts and often include interviews with industry experts, researchers, and company leaders to provide a deeper understanding of technological breakthroughs.
- Format: The podcast is available on major platforms like Apple Podcasts, Spotify, and ARK's website. Content is delivered through different series:
    - FYI – The For Your Innovation Podcast:An umbrella podcast covering general developments in innovation, featuring a variety of ARK team members as hosts and guests.
    - The Brainstorm: A series where ARK analysts discuss emerging trends and news related to innovation and tech.
    - Bitcoin Brainstorm: A dedicated series focused specifically on Bitcoin and broader cryptocurrency topics.
    - In The Know (Video Series): A monthly video series where Cathie Wood discusses broader economic trends, monetary policy, and market signals. 

Key figures involved

Cathie Wood
- Role: Founder, CEO, and CIO of ARK Invest.
- Bio: With over 40 years of experience in identifying and investing in innovation, Cathie Wood is the public face of ARK. She founded the firm in 2014 to focus exclusively on disruptive technologies. She regularly contributes to the podcast and video series, offering her high-level perspective on macro trends and investment strategy. 

Brett Winton
- Role: Chief Futurist.
- Bio: As a podcast host and frequent guest, Winton focuses on long-term technological convergence. He drives ARK's forecasts across tech, economies, and asset classes, helping to dimension the long-term impact of disruptive innovation. 

Sam Korus
- Role: Director of Research, Autonomous Technology & Robotics.
- Bio: A podcast host and guest, Korus specializes in the fields of robotics and autonomous technology. His expertise is often featured on "The Brainstorm" podcast, where he discusses advancements and market implications in these areas. 

Nicholas Grous
- Role: Director of Research, Consumer Internet and Fintech.
- Bio: A regular host and guest on the podcast, Grous offers deep insights into the consumer internet and fintech sectors. He often provides analysis on new companies and market strategies during "The Brainstorm" episodes. 

Other contributors
Many other ARK analysts and experts contribute to the podcasts, providing specialized knowledge on topics ranging from digital assets to genomics, highlighting the collaborative and research-driven nature of ARK Invest.
