Marina Wyss is a senior applied scientist at Twitch and Amazon, specializing in AI and machine learning. She is also a content creator on YouTube and Medium, where she shares her expertise in data science, machine learning, and career advice. Wyss is known for her non-traditional path into tech, starting with a background in political science and working in non-profits and political market research before transitioning into data science. 
Career and background
- Education: Wyss holds a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science from the University of California, Berkeley. She later pursued an academic focus on social data science at the Hertie School in Berlin, where she worked as a teaching assistant and data scientist.
- Career change: She transitioned into the tech industry after seven years of "relentless learning" following her political science degree. Her prior experience included running a small business, working in political campaigns, and non-profit management.
- Current role: As a senior applied scientist at Twitch/Amazon, Wyss works with generative AI, forecasting, and MLOps (Machine Learning Operations). She focuses on building production-level machine learning systems. 
Public profile and mentorship
- YouTube: Wyss operates the YouTube channel @MarinaWyssAI, where she creates videos on data science, machine learning, and professional development for an audience of over 88,000 subscribers.
- Blogging: She writes articles on Medium under the "Gratitude Driven" publication, covering topics like AI, machine learning, and productivity.
- Mentoring: Wyss has been mentoring individuals since 2022 to help them break into the tech field and provides career advice and insights on platforms like Topmate.
- Newsletter: She also publishes a newsletter covering data science, machine learning, and personal growth. 
Achievements and expertise
- Recognized expert: Wyss's work has been featured on Amazon Science and in publications like Towards Data Science.
- Certifications: She holds multiple certifications in machine learning and generative AI from Coursera and Amazon Web Services (AWS).
- Volunteer work: Her volunteer activities have included serving as a Data Ambassador for DataKind, where she worked on a computer vision model for an NGO.
