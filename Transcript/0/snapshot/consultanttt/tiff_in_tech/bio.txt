Tiffany Janzen, widely known as "Tiff in Tech," is a Canadian software developer, tech consultant, public speaker, and entrepreneur. She is the founder of TiffinTech, an online education platform dedicated to making technology accessible to a global audience. Janzen's journey into the tech world is a notable career pivot, starting in fashion before training as a developer.
Early life and career transition
- From fashion to full-stack: Janzen grew up in Saskatchewan, Canada, and initially pursued a career in fashion and modeling overseas. After returning to Canada and studying graphic communications at Ryerson University, she took an introductory coding course that ignited her passion for technology.
- Bootcamp to IBM: To facilitate her career change, she completed a coding bootcamp, which led to a role as a full-stack developer at a startup in 2017. She later advanced to become a senior software developer and technical consultant at IBM, where she also served as a mentor.
The TiffinTech platform
- Founder and content creator: In 2019, Janzen launched TiffinTech, a multi-platform educational brand that provides tech insights, career advice, and coding tutorials. The platform has since grown to more than 1.3 million followers across YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram.
- A global voice: As a prominent tech speaker, Janzen is known for making complex topics understandable and exciting. She has delivered keynotes and led panels on subjects like AI, emerging technologies, and career motivation.
- Major collaborations: TiffinTech partners with some of the biggest names in the industry, including Apple, NVIDIA, Meta, Amazon, Microsoft, and LinkedIn, to create technical content.
High-profile interviews
- Talk Tech with Tiff: Janzen is the host of the "Talk Tech with Tiff" podcast, where she interviews influential tech leaders and founders.
- Notable guests: Her interviews have featured tech icons such as Meta CEO Mark Zuckerberg and NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang, discussing the future of technology and innovation.
Continuing education and consulting
- Advanced studies: As of 2025, Janzen is pursuing graduate studies in AI and machine learning at Stanford University. She previously completed programs in AI/ML at George Brown College and full-stack development at BrainStation and HackerYou.
- Strategic consultant: She works as a tech consultant and advisor for both startups and major tech companies ("MAANG"), developing brand strategies and educational programs.
- Taekoff founder: She also founded Taekoff, a learning platform offering workshops, on-demand talks, and networking opportunities for tech professionals.
Recognition and advocacy
- Industry recognition: In 2025, she was named one of Edelman's "AI Creators You Need to Know," cementing her status as a leading voice in technology.
- Empowering women in tech: A core part of Janzen's mission is to advocate for women in technology and show that a career in tech is possible for anyone, regardless of background.
