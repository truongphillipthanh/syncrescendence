Two Bit da Vinci is the alias for Ricky Roy, the host of a popular YouTube channel and media company focused on science, engineering, and technology. The company's mission is to explain complex innovations, particularly in the green energy sector, in an accessible way for a general audience.
About Ricky Roy and his work
- Alias and Mission: The "Two Bit da Vinci" moniker reflects Roy's goal to be a modern-day Renaissance man, exploring the intersections of science, technology, and engineering, just as Leonardo da Vinci combined art and science centuries ago. The phrase "Two Bit" suggests a humble, accessible, or common-person's approach to genius.
- Content and Focus: Roy's videos delve into emerging technologies and major engineering projects. He breaks down complex subjects with fun facts and key insights, with a particular interest in green and sustainable energy solutions.
- YouTube Channel: The Two Bit da Vinci YouTube channel has amassed a large following, with hundreds of videos covering a wide range of topics. Some of his most popular content includes deep dives into significant engineering feats and disasters, such as the OceanGate submarine implosion.
- Educational approach: Roy's style is to make complex, often intimidating topics understandable and engaging. He aims to help viewers "think like an engineer" about the world's most pressing and innovative stories.
- Platforms: While best known for his YouTube content, Ricky Roy and the Two Bit da Vinci brand also maintain a presence on other social media platforms, including X (formerly Twitter) and Facebook.
