Farzad Mesbahi is a prominent YouTube content creator known for his in-depth analysis and commentary on Tesla and the broader electric vehicle (EV) industry. His unique perspective is informed by his experience as a former Tesla employee and a long-time investor.
Career and personal background
- Former Tesla employee: From 2017 to 2021, Mesbahi worked at Tesla, where he helped expand the company's parts distribution network. This insider experience gives him a unique and informed perspective on the company's operations, culture, and products.
- Long-term investor: He has been a Tesla shareholder since 2012, well before he joined the company. This provides him with a deep, long-term understanding of the company's trajectory and financial performance.
- Entrepreneurial background: Before Tesla, Mesbahi worked as a Director of Business Intelligence and Pricing at a major pet food distributor. He has also owned a small business with his wife.
- International background: Born in Spain to Iranian immigrants, Mesbahi moved to the United States in 1999. He attributes his worldly perspective to this diverse upbringing.
YouTube content and style
- Focused analysis: Farzad's content is primarily dedicated to Tesla, but he also covers Elon Musk's other ventures and broader EV market trends.
- Insider insights: He often draws upon his experiences working at Tesla to offer nuanced commentary that goes beyond what is publicly available.
- Podcast format: In addition to solo videos, he hosts the "Farzad Podcast," where he interviews guests and engages in deep discussions.
- Growth and evolution: What began as a passion project soon grew into full-fledged channel. He has since hired staff to help with content production, such as creating supercuts of his posts from X (formerly Twitter).
Business and investing
- Not financial advice: Mesbahi includes a disclaimer that his content is for entertainment and informational purposes only and should not be taken as professional financial advice.
- Revenue streams: His channel's revenue primarily comes from YouTube ads, though he also engages in some sponsorships. He maintains a hands-on approach to creating content that aligns with his genuine interests.
