Dr. Justin Sung is a former medical doctor who is now a prominent learning coach, educator, and social entrepreneur. He is known for teaching evidence-based learning and self-management strategies through his educational platform, iCanStudy, and popular YouTube channel.

Background and medical career
- Medical doctor: Sung earned his Bachelor of Medical Sciences (Honours) from the University of Auckland, graduating with first-class honors in 2016.
- Transition to educator: While still in medical school, Sung spent years teaching students who wanted to get into medicine. He eventually realized that focusing on teaching effective learning skills, rather than just subject-matter content, yielded more significant and consistent results. Finding greater fulfillment in teaching, he left his medical career to become a full-time educator.

iCanStudy
- Co-founder: Sung is the co-founder and Head of Learning at iCanStudy, an online platform dedicated to teaching higher-order thinking and self-regulated learning.
- Methodology: The platform provides programs and resources on evidence-based learning strategies and self-management methods for students and professionals. It aims to help learners master new knowledge and skills efficiently.
- Curriculum: iCanStudy offers a diagnostic assessment of learning habits, video lessons on effective learning principles, and interactive quizzes. It also features workshops with Sung, a global community, and coaching support.

Educational content and speaking
- YouTube: Sung runs a popular YouTube channel where he shares practical, evidence-based learning techniques with a wide audience. His videos explain how to process information effectively, emphasizing deep understanding and inquiry-based learning over rote memorization.
- TEDx speaker: He is a top 1% viewed TEDx speaker, having given a talk on practical learning efficiency skills and the limitations of traditional study methods.
- Guest lecturer: Sung has been a guest lecturer at Monash University, where he has taught learning skills to Master's and PhD students.
- Research: He is involved in research at Monash University, investigating how to objectively measure the effectiveness and efficiency of various learning strategies.
- Author: He has authored educational content, including a solo-authored chapter published by Springer Nature on higher-order learning.

Social entrepreneurship
- Focus on equity: As a social entrepreneur, Sung has a strong interest in educational equity.
- Non-profit work: Before co-founding iCanStudy, he ran a non-profit education program in New Zealand for seven years. This initiative focused on helping students from disadvantaged backgrounds pursue careers in healthcare.
