Tiago Forte is a leading expert in the field of productivity, digital organization, and personal knowledge management. As the founder of Forte Labs, he developed the "Building a Second Brain" methodology, a system for creating a trusted external repository for ideas and insights. 

Career and methodology
- Forte Labs: Forte founded Forte Labs, a productivity training firm and consultancy that teaches individuals and organizations how to thrive in a digital world.
- Building a Second Brain (BASB): His signature program is a comprehensive system for knowledge management. It provides a framework for capturing, organizing, and retrieving information to enhance creativity, reduce stress, and improve personal effectiveness.
- The PARA Method: A core organizational framework within BASB, PARA stands for Projects, Areas, Resources, and Archives. This system helps users categorize and manage their digital information intuitively.
- The CODE Method: Another key framework developed by Forte is CODE: Capture, Organize, Distill, and Express. This process guides users to consistently turn the information they consume into creative output and results.
- Online courses: Forte has taught his methods to thousands of people worldwide through online courses and workshops, including the popular BASB program.
- Author: He is the author of the best-selling book Building a Second Brain: A Proven Method to Organize Your Digital Life and Unlock Your Creative Potential and The PARA Method: Simplify, Organize, and Master Your Digital Life. 

Background and experience
- Diverse experience: Forte's background includes working in microfinance in Latin America and serving as an English teacher and civic activist in the Peace Corps in Ukraine.
- Early inspiration: A chronic throat illness that caused short-term memory loss led Forte to develop a digital note-taking system to remember what he had learned, which was the genesis of his work on digital productivity.
- Corporate consulting: Before starting Forte Labs, he worked as a product development consultant for large companies in San Francisco.
- Recognized expert: He has worked with companies such as Genentech and Toyota and has been featured in publications like The New York Times, Harvard Business Review, and The Atlantic.
