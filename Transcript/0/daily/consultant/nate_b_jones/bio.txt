Nate B. Jones is a former Amazon product leader and tech entrepreneur who is now a prolific educator and commentator focused on artificial intelligence. His YouTube channel, AI News & Strategy Daily, provides daily analysis on the rapidly evolving AI landscape, cutting through hype to offer actionable insights.
Career and expertise
- Diverse experience: Jones's tech career includes building AI products at Amazon and founding his own successful e-commerce business. This hands-on background informs his practical perspective on technology.
- AI strategy for leaders: He has advised CXOs and product leaders at Fortune 500 companies on how to leverage AI breakthroughs for business growth.
- A "learn by doing" philosophy: Jones advocates for a "try stuff and be okay with failing" approach to learning AI, believing the technology rewards those who are willing to jump in and experiment.
- Focus on value: A key theme in his content is that AI is useless without a clear, valuable use case. He encourages his audience to focus on finding real-world applications and iterating until they deliver tangible benefits.
YouTube and other platforms
- Content style: Jones's videos offer straight-talking, data-backed insights on AI development, focusing on strategy and actionable advice for professionals. He regularly summarizes major AI reports and news so his audience doesn't have to.
- Wider presence: Beyond his YouTube channel, Jones also provides his AI commentary on TikTok and through a Substack newsletter.
- Seattle-based: He is based in Seattle, where he is also working on a book about AI.
