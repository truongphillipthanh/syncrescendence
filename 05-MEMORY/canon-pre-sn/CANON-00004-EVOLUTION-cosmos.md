## THE DEFRAG DECISION

On December 30, 2025, the Sovereign authorized Metabolic Defrag—the most aggressive reduction in corpus history.

### The Numbers

| Metric | Before | After | Reduction |
|--------|--------|-------|-----------|
| Files | ~658 | ~136 | 79% |
| Directories | ~914 | 14 | 98% |
| Characters | ~12.7M | ~3.25M | 74% |
| Depth | 5+ | 2 | 60% |

### What Was Deleted

**Bulk Excretions**:
- `staging/` (887 files) — superseded drafts
- `annealment/` (5 files, ~134K) — historical coordination
- `evaluation/` (4 files, ~147K) — superseded assessments
- `orchestration/` (entire directory) — stale coordination state

**The Painful Losses** (Kintsugi Acknowledgment):
- `metahumanism/` (8 files, ~855K) — philosophical explorations now partially preserved in QUEUE-36000
- `autopsychography` (~90 files) — deep personal excavation writing
- `orchestration/history/` — session records from all Oracle phases
- `cognitive_palace_core/` (14 files) — implementation experiments

### Why 79% Reduction Was Correct

The framework had become:
1. **Unnavigable**: Too many files to hold in working memory
2. **Inconsistent**: Terminology and structure varied unpredictably
3. **Cruft-laden**: Historical artifacts served no active purpose
4. **Bloated**: Content repeated across multiple versions

The reduction preserved signal and eliminated noise.
