## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Overall Scaffold Coverage of Convergence Vision: ~12%**

The scaffold has evolved into a sophisticated internal coordination system for AI agent orchestration (agent loops, sovereignty strata, dispatch protocols, decision frameworks, ontology management). This internal infrastructure is world-class. However, it contains near-zero operational coverage of the outward-facing civilizational vision described in the convergence documents: the business engine, content brand, 15+ platform deployment, 4-stage curriculum, economic model, live events, physical field nodes, community governance, compliance frameworks, and 10-year phased timeline.

The scaffold operationalizes the HOW of AI coordination but not the WHAT of the endeavor. This is not a failure -- it reflects the correct build order (build the factory before the product). But the audit reveals the scaffold is mature enough that the next development phase should be **product-facing scaffold injection**.

**Convergence documents describe**: A complete 10-15 year pathway from abstract frameworks through content creation, economic sustainability, live events, physical infrastructure (Field Nodes), to distributed network of Way Places at civilizational scale.

**Scaffold currently captures**: AI constellation coordination, tool stack management, ontological architecture, decision procedures, terminology reconciliation, and internal operational doctrine.
