## STRUCTURAL OBSERVATIONS

### 1. Domain Gap
The Rosetta Stone v2.6.0 is heavily weighted toward **internal scaffold operations** (agent orchestration, corpus management, SN encoding, ontological architecture). The convergence documents define the **outward-facing strategic vision** (business engine, content strategy, educational curriculum, physical infrastructure). These are complementary domains. The Rosetta Stone currently has 17 categories; a new **Category 18: Convergence Architecture** and **Category 19: Content Strategy** would logically house the missing terms.

### 2. Anti-Pattern Gap
The convergence documents contain specific named anti-patterns ("Cohorts before Patreon," "Platform sprawl," "Architectural churn," "Unlogged claims," "Scope creep") that function as negative space definitions. The Rosetta Stone has the Sophistication Plateau (#227) as a named anti-pattern but none of the convergence anti-patterns. These deserve their own sub-section.

### 3. Philosophical Gap
The convergence documents have a rich philosophical vocabulary (Consciousness Investigating Itself, Recursive Amplification, Gaialogical Principles, Voluntary Coordination, Distributed Resilience, Integrative Interregnum, Demonstration Principle) that is almost entirely absent from the Rosetta Stone. The Rosetta Stone has Sovereignty (#185) as the only philosophical entry. A **Category 20: Convergence Philosophy** is warranted.

### 4. Economic Gap
The convergence documents articulate a complete economic philosophy and model (Risk Removed / Time Returned, Content-Native Sustainability, Surplus Accumulation, Capital Strategy, six pricing tiers, unit economics, sensitivity analysis). The Rosetta Stone's Financial Domain (#10, terms 179-183) captures metaphorical usage (Anneal, Amortize, Compound, Dividend, Sunk Cost) but none of the actual business model terminology.

### 5. Community/Events Gap
The entire community architecture (Steward, Proof Session, Quarterly Gathering, Annual Convergence, Special Intensive, Peer Coils, Run-of-Show, Consent Framework) is absent from the Rosetta Stone. No Rosetta category covers this domain.

### 6. Tri-Helix Confusion Risk
Entry #16 deprecates "Tri-Helix" as Chain Matrix terminology. The convergence "Tri-Helical Strategy" is a completely different concept (three synchronized timelines). This creates a dangerous false-negative: someone searching for the convergence concept would find only "DEPRECATED" without realizing the active strategic framework exists under a near-identical name. Resolution is critical.
