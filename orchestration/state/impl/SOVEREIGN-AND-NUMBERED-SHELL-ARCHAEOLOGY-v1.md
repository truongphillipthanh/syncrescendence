## 3. The Missing `03`

There is no stable top-level `03-*` lane in the numbered-era snapshot.

Observed facts:
- root has `00`, `01`, `02`, `04`, `05`
- `memory/` exists but is empty
- no top-level `03-*` directory is present

Most responsible reading:
- the hierarchy was semantically intentional, but not fully completed
- `03` was either never materialized or was absorbed into other layers before it stabilized
- it should not be retroactively hallucinated into certainty

So for successor-shell purposes:
- the numbering pattern is real and meaningful
- but the absence of `03` is itself evidence of incomplete institutional consolidation

## 5. What the Numbering Meant

The numbering was not just “pin important folders to the top.”

It encoded order of semantic dependency:

- `00` = coordinate the present
- `01` = protect the truth
- `02` = hold the machinery
- `04` = store the feedstock
- `05` = compact into operational wisdom

This is why the old shell still felt coherent despite its clutter.
The root naming was doing semantic work.

The problem was that later drift broke the distinction between:

- executive and log
- apparatus and communication
- source and archive
- wisdom and active law

## 6. How This Informs the Successor Shell

The successor shell should preserve the semantic hierarchy without preserving the topological debt.

### Preserve

1. `00-ORCHESTRATION`'s sense of live coordination and protected state
2. `01-CANON`'s sense of defended truth
3. `02-ENGINE`'s sense of apparatus and composable machinery
4. `04-SOURCES`'s sense of intake/feedstock
5. `05-SIGMA`'s sense of operational wisdom and compaction
6. `-SOVEREIGN`'s executive-audit function

### Do not preserve

1. dashed root names
2. executive and escalation traffic mixed in one unlabeled lane
3. apparatus files and communications files mixed by convenience
4. reliance on numbering alone to enforce semantics

The new shell should not forget the numbering logic.

---

## 8. Net Judgment

The old numbered shell was one of the clearest signs that the project already knew what kind of institution it was trying to become.

The later shells obscured that with:

- duplicate topologies
- ad hoc root lanes
- mixed artifact classes
- incomplete filing law

But the semantics survived in:

- `-SOVEREIGN`
- the numbered layer meanings
- the summit reports
- the old AGENTS and COCKPIT documents

So the correct successor-shell move is not to discard the numbering thesis.
It is to internalize its semantic distinctions into the new federal shell, where lane law is explicit and topology debt is no longer required to express meaning.
