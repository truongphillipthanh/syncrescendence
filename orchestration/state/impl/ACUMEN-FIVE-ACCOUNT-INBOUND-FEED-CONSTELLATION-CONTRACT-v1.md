## Constitutional Rule

The governed inbound order is:

`five chain emails -> per-account curated manifest capture -> import preview / admission discipline -> Acumen portfolio or registry admission -> Acumen poll and triage -> downstream derivative handoff`

Only Acumen may own the shared raw intake and triage plane.
`coherence`, `efficacy`, `mastery`, and `transcendence` may own curated manifests and propose imports, but they may not operate parallel raw intake runtimes, shadow registries, or bypass Acumen admission.

## Truth Classes

The following truth classes must remain distinct:

1. platform follow / subscription state
2. captured per-account source-of-truth manifest
3. imported Acumen portfolio or registry state
4. Acumen poll, triage, verification, and product artifacts
5. later-chain derivative synthesis or execution artifacts

A source being followed by one of the five accounts does not automatically make it live Acumen intake.
Shared operational intake begins only after explicit Acumen admission.

## Role Law

1. `acumen` is the only active shared intake and triage authority.
2. `coherence`, `efficacy`, `mastery`, and `transcendence` are upstream curated curation surfaces in this inbound contract.
3. Those four later chains may also become downstream consumers of Acumen derivative outputs, but that later-chain role does not grant raw-intake authority.
4. Import from a non-Acumen account is lawful only after capture, identity certainty, and explicit Acumen admission.
5. No later chain may materialize its own raw-intake runtime directory, polling authority, or registry authority as part of this constellation.

## Lawful Overlap

Overlap between account manifests is allowed only when the chain rationale differs and is recorded.
The same source may lawfully appear in multiple manifests if each appearance serves a distinct role such as:

1. `frontier_signal`
   - current consequential monitoring for Acumen
2. `framework_depth`
   - conceptual lineage or synthesis depth for Coherence
3. `implementation_relevance`
   - build, operator, or workflow relevance for Efficacy
4. `pedagogical_exemplar`
   - explanation quality or curriculum value for Mastery
5. `meta_governance`
   - long-horizon orientation, civilizational meaning, or doctrine pressure for Transcendence

Example:

`frontier lab channel`

1. may belong to `acumen` for early-signal monitoring
2. may belong to `coherence` for framework implications
3. may belong to `efficacy` for implementation consequences

That is lawful overlap.
Unlabeled duplication is not.

3. downstream chain work and dissemination remain derivative of that governed intake rather than alternative authorities
