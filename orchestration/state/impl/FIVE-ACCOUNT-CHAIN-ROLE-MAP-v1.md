## Overlap Examples

The same source may be present in more than one upstream manifest when the chain rationale stays explicit.

1. frontier lab or research source
   - `acumen` for consequential monitoring
   - `coherence` for conceptual implications
   - `efficacy` for build impact
2. exceptional teacher or explainer
   - `coherence` for framework clarity
   - `mastery` for pedagogical method
   - `efficacy` when the explanation is directly buildable
3. long-horizon philosopher or governance source
   - `coherence` for synthesis depth
   - `transcendence` for meta-governance and meaning
   - `acumen` only when a current consequential signal exists

Overlap without rationale is drift.
Overlap with explicit rationale is lawful specialization.
