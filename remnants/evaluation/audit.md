- Mitigation:
  - Enforce prerequisite completion
  - Regular "ground truth" checking with therapists/coaches
  - Community accountability for authentic vs. performative practice

**Grandiosity and Delusion**
- Risk: 10-100x capability claims feeding narcissistic inflation
- Warning signs: Practitioner claims abilities without demonstration, resists feedback, creates personality cult
- Mitigation:
  - Empirical validation requirements
  - Regular reality-testing protocols
  - Community evaluation frameworks
  - Falsification criteria for all claimed breakthroughs

### B. Community Risks

**Cult Dynamics**
- Risk: Mystery + Mythology + Initiation = traditional cult recipe
- Warning signs: Leader worship, us-vs-them thinking, information control, financial exploitation
- Mitigation:
  - No central authority or guru figure
  - Transparent operations and finances
  - Explicit exit protocols (leaving is celebrated, not punished)
  - Diversity of interpretation encouraged
  - Fork culture embraced
  - Regular "orthopraxy not orthodoxy" reminders

**Schism and Fragmentation**
- Risk: Multiple valid interpretations → potential community splits
- Cause: Different practitioners resonate with different dialectical frameworks
- Mitigation:
  - Frame diversity as strength not weakness
  - Create "big tent" culture
  - Celebrate parallel experiments
  - Shared empirical standards across interpretations
  - Annual convergence events maintaining connection

**Elitism and Gatekeeping**
- Risk: Tier 2+ restrictions create insider/outsider dynamics
- Warning signs: "Advanced practitioners" looking down on beginners, artificial complexity inflation
- Mitigation:
  - Frame tiers as safety protocols not status markers
  - Emphasize beginners maintain valuable "beginner's mind"
  - Advanced practitioners serve beginners (no hierarchy)
  - Regular "return to basics" practice for everyone

### C. Technical Risks

**Complexity Explosion**
- Risk: Adding enhancement layers (shadow work, mysteries, dialectics) overwhelming accessibility
- Current state: Already at complexity limit (148,000 words)
- Mitigation:
  - Keep Tier 0 radically simple (Seven Pulses only)
  - Progressive disclosure of complexity
  - Multiple entry points at different sophistication levels
  - "Dimensional folding" creating multiple views

**Technology Dependency**
- Risk: System so AI-integrated that technology failure cripples practice
- Scenario: Modal 2 AI capabilities plateau or regress
- Mitigation:
  - Maintain analog-first capability foundation
  - Regular "technology sabbath" practice days
  - Tool transcendence protocols
  - Human capability never fully delegated to AI

**Temporal Brittleness**
- Risk: Modal Sequence assumes steady AI advancement; disruption breaks architecture
