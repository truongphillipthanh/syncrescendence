# GENESIS: The Narrative Substrate

---

## Purpose

GENESIS provides what CANON cannot: **mythology beneath cosmology**.

CANON documents articulate the architecture—what the system IS and how it WORKS. GENESIS documents articulate the narrative—WHY the system exists, WHERE it comes from, WHAT it teaches in compressed form, and HOW it arrived at its current state.

The distinction matters:
- **CANON** = structure (eternal, versioned discretely)
- **GENESIS** = story (timeless, living, accumulated)

Both are load-bearing. Remove either and the system becomes incomprehensible.

## Relationship to CANON

GENESIS documents are **not CANON**. They:
- Do not require the same version control rigor
- Accumulate rather than version discretely
- Prioritize narrative over specification
- May be subjective where CANON must be precise

GENESIS documents **integrate with CANON** through:
- Cross-references to canonical architecture
- Shared vocabulary and concepts
- Complementary coverage (story + structure)
- Mutual illumination

---

## The Layer's Function

In cosmological terms, GENESIS operates as **Reality Layer (Layer 0)** for the entire system—the ontological bedrock and axiological foundation upon which all else builds.

If CANON is the architecture, GENESIS is the ground it stands on.

If CANON is the what and how, GENESIS is the why and whence.

If CANON speaks to the analytical mind, GENESIS speaks to imagination and soul.

Both modes describe identical reality from complementary angles. Neither superior. Both necessary.
