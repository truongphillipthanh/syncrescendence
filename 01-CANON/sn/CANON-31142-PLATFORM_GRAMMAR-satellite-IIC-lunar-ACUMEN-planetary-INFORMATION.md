## GLOSS

Positioned at Layer 3 of the Constellation Architecture: Feedcraft tells you WHAT platforms ARE (Layer 1), IIC Constellation tells you WHICH accounts use WHICH platforms (Layer 2), Platform Grammar tells you EXACTLY HOW TO CONFIGURE AND INTERACT with each platform. Provides subscription management philosophies, notification strategies, interaction protocols for both personal (invisible) and organizational (strategic visibility) IICs, plus enshittification warning signals with exit strategies.

### NORM::XTwitterProtocol
```
sutra: Real-time discourse | surveillance + synthesis | high lock-in risk

spec:
  platform_character:
    function: real_time_discourse | breaking_news | short_form
    model: advertising + premium
    ethos: speed | brevity | discourse | memetic
    lock_in: HIGH (audience | discourse_dependence | FOMO)

  following_strategy:
    mistake: "following everyone into main timeline"
    approach: "Lists organization | ZERO main timeline consumption"

    list_architecture:
      primary_sources_20_30: frontier_CEOs | research_leads | company_accounts
      expert_synthesizers_50_80: deep_threads | domain_expertise | polymaths
      informed_commentary_80_120: industry_analysts | adjacent_experts | thoughtful
      speculative_horizon_30_50: futurists | contrarians | adjacent_possibility
      personal_separate: friends | mentors | ZERO professional_mixing

    decision_criteria:
      - signal_to_noise_ratio
      - thread_quality (structured | not_reactive)
      - domain_expertise (genuine_depth | not_performative)
      - temporal_relevance (breaking_valuable | not_rehashing)
      - ego_to_insight_ratio

  notification_strategy:
    ON: 5-10_accounts_MAX | Sam_Altman | Dario | Jensen | critical_researchers
    OFF: everyone_else | check_Lists_manually | disable_algorithmic

  interaction_protocols:
    personal_IICs:
      post: NEVER (accounts_dormant)
      like: strategic_bookmarking_ONLY (trains algorithm)
      retweet: NEVER
      reply: NEVER
      quote_tweet: NEVER
      bookmark: organize_by_Chain + Phase + Content_Type
      algorithm_training: intentional_clicking >> signal

    organizational_IICs:
      following: ecosystem_members | 80%_relevant | 20%_adjacent
      posting_strategy:
        intelligence_threads: 2-3x/week | pattern_recognition | 5-12_tweets
        framework_explanations: 1-2x/week | concepts_accessible | 3-8_tweets
        implementation_insights: as_needed | tool_tips | 1-3_tweets
        vision_purpose: monthly | why_matters | 3-5_tweets

      interaction:
        like: generously (20-50/day | support_ecosystem)
        retweet: selectively (3-8/day | add_context)
        reply: thoughtfully (add_value | not_just_"this!")
        quote_tweet: rarely (substantial_perspective_only)
        DMs: professional_networking | respond_24-48h

      engagement_timing:
        post: 6-9AM_PT | 11AM-2PM_PT | 5-7PM_PT
        threads: Mon-Thu (Fri-Sun lower)
        responses: within_1-2hr (algorithm_rewards)

  content_format:
    thread_structure:
      tweet_1: hook + clear_thesis
      tweet_2_3: context (why_matters)
      tweet_4_8: evidence/analysis (core_argument)
      tweet_9_10: implications (so_what?)
      tweet_11: CTA (link | question)
      tweet_12: summary + resources

    visual_integration: images | charts | diagrams | avoid_stock_photos
    formatting: line_breaks | numbers | bold_sparingly | questions_engage

  algorithm_optimization:
    prioritizes: engagement_rate (replies > RT > likes) | time_on_platform | recency | authority
    penalizes: external_clicks

    optimize_without_compromise:
      YES: structure_for_readability | ask_thoughtful_questions | peak_hours | engage_own_replies
      NO: clickbait | engagement_bait | external_links_in_first_tweet | generic_responses

  enshittification_signals:
    - increasing_bot_spam
    - algorithm >> controversy > quality
    - arbitrary_shadowbanning
    - degrading_search
    - paywalling_core_features

  exit_strategy:
    - email_list_maintained
    - follower_export_if_API
    - cross_post_Substack
    - personal_site_canonical
```

### NORM::SubstackProtocol
```
sutra: Long-form synthesis | owned audience | low-medium lock-in

spec:
  platform_character:
    function: newsletter | long_form_written
    model: subscription (10% take)
    ethos: independent_voices | direct_audience | depth
    lock_in: low_medium (email_portable | content_exportable | habits)

  subscription_management:
    mistake: "subscribing to every interesting writer"
    approach: "curated depth > breadth | quality utterly dominates"

    criteria:
      - synthesis_quality
      - epistemic_rigor
      - signal_density
      - temporal_relevance
      - unique_perspective

    categories:
      weekly_5_8: high_signal | frequent | directly_relevant
      monthly_10_15: less_frequent | deep | framework_building
      archive_15_20: timeless | reference | selective_read
      unsubscribe_ruthlessly: drifted | irrelevant | better_found | never_read

  reading_strategy:
    mistake: "trying to read everything"
    approach: "AI-prescreening + batch processing"

    AI_prescreening:
      weekly_saturday: 30-60min
      process:
        1: Claude >> 3_key_insights + surprising_claim
        2: assess >> full_read_justified?
        3: categorize >> MUST_READ | EXTRACT | ARCHIVE | DELETE
        4: schedule >> dedicated_reading_time

    batch_reading:
      when: Saturday_morning | 2-4hr
      environment: no_distractions | notebook_open
      process: read >> annotate >> synthesize >> extract
      integration: connect_existing | cross_domain_patterns

  interaction_protocols:
    personal_IICs:
      comments: NEVER
      likes: NEVER
      shares: save_for_org_content
      subscriptions: free_tiers_only

    organizational_IICs:
      publication_strategy:
        frequency: weekly_initially
        length: 1500-3000_words
        format: hook >> framework >> synthesis >> CTA

      content_sections:
        intelligence_brief: acumen@ | pattern_recognition
        framework_deep_dive: coherence@ | 60-70%
        implementation_guide: efficacy@ | practical
        teaching_insight: mastery@ | quarterly
        vision_purpose: transcendence@ | quarterly

      monetization_Scaling+:
        free_80%: intel_brief + teasers
        paid_$8-15/mo: full_frameworks | implementation | Q&A
        founding_$150-300/yr: behind_scenes | early_access | consultation_discount

  growth_strategy:
    traffic_sources:
      X: "short version [thread] | long version [link]"
      YouTube: end_screen | pinned_comment | description
      LinkedIn: excerpt + link
      Reddit: answer_questions | link_when_helpful

    cross_promotion:
      X_thread >> Substack_essay: weekly
      YouTube >> Substack_transcript: bi_weekly
      Substack >> YouTube: monthly_major_frameworks

    email_list:
      export: quarterly
      segment: active | inactive | never_opened
      re_engage: campaign_or_prune

  enshittification_signals:
    - increasing_fees
    - forced_Notes_usage
    - algorithm >> platform > subscriber
    - export_restrictions
    - arbitrary_moderation

  exit_strategy:
    - content_in_Markdown_BEFORE_publish
    - email_list_exported
    - Ghost_self_hosted_path
    - subscribers_informed_in_advance
```
