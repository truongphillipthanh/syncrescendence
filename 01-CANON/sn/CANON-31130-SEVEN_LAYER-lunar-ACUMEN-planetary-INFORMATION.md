## SUTRA

Seven-layer architecture enables differential sovereignty—intentional coupling at layers where benefits justify costs, maintained independence at layers protecting core autonomy.

---

## GLOSS

Technology stack now mediates cognition—not neutral substrate but intelligence substrate. Every layer (device→browser→application→platform) accumulates context about you. Switching costs are cognitive, not technical. Losing an AI browser means losing an intelligence partner that knows you. This architecture provides decision framework for intentional coupling vs. strategic independence across seven layers, enabling sustained sovereignty amid pervasive intelligencing while capitalizing on ecosystem economies of scale.
