## PART III: DEPENDENCY NAVIGATION

### Critical Prerequisites by Degree

**Degree: 1-Recognition**  
*No prerequisites*‚Äîpure introduction

**Degree: 2-Exploration**  
- Read minimum 3 source artifacts (choose by pathway)
- Establish basic daily practice (20+ min)
- Find/form study group (peer learning)

**Degree: 3-Commitment**  
- Complete all 6 categories in chosen system (Coherence or Transcendence)
- Demonstrate sustained practice (6+ months)
- Document personal insights/methodology
- Mentor at least one Recognition-level practitioner

**Degree: 4-Integration**  
- Mastery in 1-2 domain categories (deep expertise)
- Teaching others to Degree: 2 minimum (effectiveness demonstrated)
- Personal framework contribution
- Level: 2-Intermediate minimum in at least 2 causal chains

**Degree: 5-Transmission**  
- Second-generation teaching (your students teach others)
- Framework evolution participation (new artifacts/insights)
- Wisdom council contribution (community stewardship)
- Scale: 2-Meso minimum (multi-domain sophistication)

## PART IV: WEEKLY NAVIGATION SCHEDULE

### Recognition Phase (Weeks 1-8)

**Weekly Time**: 8-10 hours total

- **Mondays**: Foundational reading (2 hours)
- **Tues-Wed**: Practice engagement (2 hours each, 4 total)
- **Thursday**: Reflection/journaling (1 hour)
- **Fri-Sun**: Integration into life (2 hours distributed)

### Exploration Phase (Weeks 9-24)

**Weekly Time**: 10-15 hours total

- **Core practice**: 45 min daily (5.25 hours/week)
- **Study cycle**: 5 hours/week (one category deep dive)
- **Community**: 2-3 hours/week (study group, discussions)
- **Integration projects**: 2 hours/week (application experiments)

### Commitment Phase (Months 6-18)

**Weekly Time**: 15-20 hours total

- **Sustained practice**: 60+ min daily (7 hours/week)
- **Deep category work**: 5-7 hours/week (two categories mastered)
- **Teaching preparation**: 2-3 hours/week (preparing to guide others)
- **Integration projects**: 3-5 hours/week (applied synthesis work)
- **Community service**: 2-3 hours/week (wisdom council, mentoring)
