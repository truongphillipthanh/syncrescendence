## DECISION GROUNDING FRAMEWORK

When strategic questions arise, reference tri-helical logic:

### Question: "Should I create video content now?"
**Check Timeline 1** (Technology): Is Vision AI mature enough? (40% now â†’ crossing threshold Month 6-9)
**Check Timeline 2** (Business): Does current phase require it? (Foundation = no, Scaling = yes)
**Check Timeline 3** (Personal): Do I have visual production competence? (If yes, wait for tools; if no, develop skills)
**Answer**: Prepare Month 1-6, begin production Month 6-9 when technology crosses threshold AND business phase supports it

### Question: "When can I teach Stage 3 curriculum?"
**Check Timeline 3** (Personal): Do I have physical systems competence? (HVAC mastery Year 2+)
**Check Timeline 2** (Business): Is business phase appropriate? (Institute Phase Year 2+)
**Check Timeline 1** (Technology): Are enabling tools mature? (Physical AI 30%+ by Year 2)
**Answer**: Year 2 earliest when ALL THREE timelines align

### Question: "Why isn't revenue scaling faster?"
**Check Timeline 2** (Business): What phase am I in? (Foundation/Validation have limited revenue by design)
**Check Timeline 3** (Personal): What can I authentically teach? (Current capability gates offerings)
**Check Timeline 1** (Technology): What tools enable which revenue streams? (Text AI enables current offerings)
**Answer**: Revenue scales with demonstrated capability, can't skip development phases

### Question: "Should I build physical Field Nodes now?"
**Check Timeline 1** (Technology): Is Physical AI accessible? (20% mature = NO)
**Check Timeline 2** (Business): Is capital accumulated? (Foundation/Validation = NO)
**Check Timeline 3** (Personal): Do I have construction competence? (Year 3+ required)
**Answer**: NO on all three dimensions. Plan now, execute Year 3-5 when timelines align

## PRACTICAL APPLICATION

### Weekly Decision Check
1. What timeline dimension does this decision depend on?
2. Are all relevant timelines aligned for this action?
3. If not aligned, what's blocking? (Technology, Business phase, Personal capability)
4. When will alignment occur? (Be specific, use timeline coordinates)
5. What preparation enables readiness? (Skills to develop, systems to build)

### Monthly Strategic Review
1. Where am I on Technology timeline? (What's mature, what's emerging)
2. Where am I on Business timeline? (What phase, what objectives)
3. Where am I on Personal timeline? (What capability, what developing)
4. Are my current activities aligned with all three? (Honest assessment)
5. What needs adjustment? (Realignment actions)

### Quarterly Vision Refresh
1. Have external timelines shifted? (Technology accelerating/decelerating)
2. Is business progression on track? (Phase objectives meeting/missing)
3. Is personal development proceeding? (Capability advancing/stagnating)
4. Do long-term plans still align? (Year 2-5 targets still realistic)
5. What strategic pivots required? (Major realignments needed)
