# Source Ingestion Protocol v1

This protocol is compacted from the archived `04-SOURCES` scaffolding and preserved specimens in [knowledge/feedstock/protocols/legacy-sources](/Users/system/syncrescendence/knowledge/feedstock/protocols/legacy-sources).

## Purpose

Convert external material into lawful shell knowledge without letting raw source mass pollute live constitutional or playbook lanes.

## Stages

1. `feedstock/raw`
   Raw captures, transcripts, dumps, or inbound source bundles.
2. `feedstock/formatted`
   Source material normalized into a readable structured form.
3. `references`
   Distilled materials worthy of active consultation.
4. `canon`
   Promoted domain knowledge with explicit lineage.

## Rules

- raw source mass does not enter `communications/`
- source templates and frontmatter contracts belong in `knowledge/feedstock/protocols/`
- integration into `knowledge/canon/`, `playbooks/`, or `validated-patterns/` should leave a traceable lineage path
