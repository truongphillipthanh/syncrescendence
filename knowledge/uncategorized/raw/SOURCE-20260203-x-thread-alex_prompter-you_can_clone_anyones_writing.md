- Content type: [essay/email/post]
- Key message: [what you want to say]

**Maintain authenticity. Don't caricature."**

What I kept:
- Short sentences that hit like punches
- Concrete details (4 hours to 8 hours, 20 minutes to 3 hours)
- Physical/temporal grounding (mornings, meetings, deadlines)
- The "shit" rhythm for accumulation
- Understatement that carries weight

## The secret sauce in Step 2:

**"Don't caricature"**

Without this, AI exaggerates quirks.

Hemingway → every sentence becomes 3 words

Paul Graham → "surprisingly" appears 47 times

**"Don't caricature" keeps it realistic.**

Subtle mimicry > obvious imitation.

## Pro tips for 99% accuracy:

1. Use 5+ samples (more data = better clone)
2. Analyze recent work (voices evolve)
3. Include variety (essays, emails, tweets)
4. Note what they DON'T do (equally important)
5. Test on people who know the original voice

Tip #4 is underrated.
