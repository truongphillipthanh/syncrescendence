### Why launchd, Not cron

macOS deprecated cron. launchd is the native daemon manager with superpowers:
- **WatchPaths**: React to file changes (our watchers use this)
- **StartCalendarInterval**: Scheduled execution (our health check uses this)
- **KeepAlive**: Auto-restart on crash (all our services use this)
- **ProcessType**: Background/Adaptive QoS (critical for MBA battery)
- **LowPriorityIO**: Reduced disk impact (MBA-specific)

On laptops, macOS power management kills cron jobs during sleep. launchd services resume gracefully. For Psyche on the MacBook Air, this is non-negotiable.
