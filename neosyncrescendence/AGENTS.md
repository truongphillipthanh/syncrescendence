# Neosyncrescendence — Sandbox Operational Law

**Status**: staging constitution  
**Purpose**: govern the shell-reconstitution sandbox without bleeding redesign work back into the legacy repo by default

## Identity

This directory is the sandbox microcosm for the next Syncrescendent shell.

It is not the historical repo in miniature by accident. It is a deliberate redesign substrate where the future shell is staged before lossless migration.

## Five Sandbox Invariants

1. **Sandbox First** — redesign work should land here unless a migration step explicitly targets the legacy repo.
2. **Pedigree Preserved** — the legacy repo remains provenance and evidence; nothing is treated as “obsolete” until lineage is captured.
3. **One Artifact, One Class** — prompts, responses, handoffs, playbooks, operators, executive artifacts, program artifacts, and assessments must stay in their lanes.
4. **Root Cleanliness** — only constitutional root surfaces, root config, and explicit lane roots belong at the root.
5. **Compile the Win** — repeated successful work should compact into playbooks, operators, or law instead of remaining as scattered threads or one-off artifacts.

## Lane Roles

- `communications/`: prompts, responses, handoffs, logs, retros, assessments
- `executive/`: live intention and steering surfaces
- `program/`: backlog, tranches, implementation sequencing
- `offices/`: agent- and harness-local offices under federal law
- `playbooks/`: harness- and surface-specific doctrine
- `operators/`: validators, packet/bridge operators, staging tools
- `runtime/`: copied runtime truth from working systems
- `pedigree/`: constitutional ancestry and unresolved heritage anchors
- `validated-patterns/`: staged “what worked” patterns from the legacy shell

## Working Rule

When in doubt:
- keep durable redesign work here
- keep evidence and ancestry traceable
- avoid writing new shell law back into legacy lanes
- promote repeated wins into doctrine quickly

## Migration Rule

This sandbox is not an isolated fantasy branch.

It is the place where:
- the target shell is clarified,
- the filing law is enforced,
- and migration-ready structure is built

before any broad repopulation of the historical repo.
