## V. INTENTIONAL NAMING PROTOCOL: INTEGRATED DEVELOPMENT

### A. The Naming Vision

**Current State**: Descriptive technical names (SEVEN_LAYER_STACK, FIVE_ACCOUNT_ARCHITECTURE, PLATFORM_GRAMMAR)

**Target State**: Intentional single-word or compound names that capture essence poetically (like Alpha Centauri vs HD 149026 b)

**Principle**: Names should resonate cosmologically, be memorable, and convey the object's nature through sound and meaning.

### B. Naming Methodology

**During refactoring**, for each document being resynthesized:

1. **Understand essence**: What is this object's true function/nature?
2. **Generate candidates**: 5-10 potential names (single words, compounds, neologisms)
3. **Test resonance**: Which name feels inevitable for this object?
4. **Check uniqueness**: No collision with existing names
5. **Validate cosmologically**: Does it fit the level (Satellary names differ from Cosmiary names)

**Criteria for good names**:
- **Memorability**: Easy to recall and pronounce
- **Resonance**: Sounds cosmologically appropriate
- **Clarity**: Conveys essence without explanation
- **Beauty**: Aesthetically pleasing (this matters)
- **Uniqueness**: Distinctive within corpus
