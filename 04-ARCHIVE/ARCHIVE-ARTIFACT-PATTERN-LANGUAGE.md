## META-DESIGN PRINCIPLES

### Principle 1: Computational Leverage
Every artifact amplifies computational power rather than human planning. Focus on search, optimization, and discovery rather than prescription.

### Principle 2: Minimal Viable Depth
Simple enough to implement, deep enough to reward continued exploration. Easy entry, infinite depth.

### Principle 3: Interoperability
Standardized interfaces, no vendor lock-in, no platform dependence, no irreplaceable components.

### Principle 4: Evolutionary Pressure
Artifacts compete for relevance. Unused artifacts deprecate. Successful patterns propagate. The canon evolves or dies.

### Principle 5: Accessible Asymptote
Democratic floor, meritocratic ceiling. Basic implementation for anyone; advanced rewards expertise.
