Caleb Writes Code is a YouTube channel run by programmer Caleb Curry, known for his AI-focused content, educational videos, and editorial insights on the tech industry. Curry is also the creator of the popular, more general programming channel Caleb Curry and the founder of the mentorship company CodeBreakthrough.
About the channel and creator
- Content focus: The channel, @CalebWritesCode, specializes in artificial intelligence, offering viewers a mix of educational and editorial content. Recent video topics include deep-dive explainers, industry commentary, and AI-related career advice.
- A decade of experience: Curry has been creating educational programming content online for over 10 years. He started his programming journey out of curiosity in high school and taught himself through extensive exploration.
- From teaching to mentoring: While his larger channel, Caleb Curry, is dedicated to beginner-friendly tutorials, he founded CodeBreakthrough to offer more direct, in-depth career mentorship for developers.
- Industry experience: Beyond YouTube, Curry has worked in various software development roles, including as a co-founder for the crypto project Space Pool and as a Head Mentor at CodeBreakthrough. He also has experience in developer relations and has worked with companies such as IBM.
