Amit Kukreja is a New York-based finance media content creator, entrepreneur, and retail investor. He is a frequent collaborator with other YouTube personalities like Farzad Mesbahi, discussing tech, business, and stock market topics.
Business and investing background
- Content creation: Kukreja hosts a popular YouTube channel called "Amit Investing". He also hosts daily live streams covering the stock market's opening and closing hours.
- Company focus: While he covers various tech and business news, his content often provides deep dives into companies like Palantir and Tesla.
- Entrepreneurship: Kukreja is the founder of Audea, a tech company that aims to help audio creators gain more discoverability. He is also the publisher of Palantir Daily, a newsletter providing analysis on the company Palantir.
- Education: He is a graduate of Rutgers Business School, with a degree in business management.
On-screen persona and content
- Interview host: Kukreja has interviewed a number of high-profile figures in finance and technology, including Vlad Tenev of Robinhood and Sham Sankar of Palantir.
- Market analysis: His content provides insight into market trends and the performance of specific stocks, with a focus on AI's impact on industries.
- Not financial advice: As with his colleagues, Kukreja includes a disclaimer that his content is for informational purposes and should not be considered professional financial advice.
