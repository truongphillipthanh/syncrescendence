About the podcast
- Focus: The podcast explains how AI is changing our world in an hour-long, engaging format. It avoids complex jargon to inform both AI enthusiasts and newcomers.
- Content: In each episode, the hosts perform hands-on demonstrations and offer practical tips, giving listeners a real sense of what AI can do. They cover topics ranging from AI automation's impact on the workforce to developments from major tech companies like OpenAI and Google.
- Unique approach: The hosts are known for exploring new tech with humor and a strong sense of fun. This dynamic keeps audiences entertained while making complex AI topics easy to digest.
- Praise: Fast Company has called it "the most entertaining way to learn about artificial intelligence".
About the hosts
Kevin Pereira
Kevin Pereira is a long-standing figure in digital media, having hosted and produced more than 1,500 hours of live television for G4tv.
- Early Career: He was the host and executive producer of G4tv's pop-culture variety program, Attack of the Show!.
- Post-G4tv: After G4, his production companies created content for major outlets, including Magnolia, Amazon, and Discovery+.
- Recent projects: Pereira has also hosted projects like the game show Let's Ask America and the DIY show Hack My Life.
- Entrepreneurship: In addition to media, he has founded multiple companies in the emerging tech space, like Web3 and AI.
Gavin Purcell
An Emmy Award-winning producer, Gavin Purcell has helped guide some of the biggest names in television into the digital age.
- Late-night television: Purcell worked as a producer on Late Night with Jimmy Fallon and is credited with helping transition the show's content into the social media world. He won multiple Emmys for his work on the franchise.
- Digital media expert: His career also includes experience at Vox Media and G4tv.
- Visionary: He frequently gives talks on the future of media, particularly regarding how AI will shape the industry in the coming years.
