## The Definition Problem

AGI resists clean definition. The corpus captures competing framings:

- **Functional (Grady/Huang)**: "AGI is the ability to figure things out. That's it." Three ingredients: knowledge (pre-training), reasoning (inference-time compute), iteration (long-horizon agents). All three now exist (00023).
- **Levels framework (Shane Legg/DeepMind)**: Minimal AGI through full AGI as a spectrum, not a binary threshold (09656).
- **Economic (Suleyman)**: AGI arrives when AI agents can be "hired" — performing economically meaningful work autonomously (09709).
- **Cognitive (Israetel)**: "An intelligence significantly more capable than the smartest humans at almost every cognitive domain" (08479).
- **Shifting goalposts**: What seemed sufficient in 2020 is now baseline. "AGI is here and it doesn't matter anymore" (Norvig, 01365). Each capability arrival triggers redefinition rather than acknowledgment.

## The METR Exponential

METR tracks the duration of real-world tasks AI can complete autonomously (00023, 00257):
- One year ago: ~10 minutes
- Recent: ~5 hours (human expert equivalent)
- Doubling every ~7 months (possibly accelerating to 4 months)
- Projected: reliable full-day work by 2028, full-year by 2034

If the exponential holds — and it has held for years — the capability trajectory is more reliable than any individual prediction. "If you extend the trend, agents should be able to work reliably to complete tasks that take human experts a full day by 2028, a full century by 2037" (00023).

## The February 2026 Inflection

Multiple sources identify a specific inflection point around February 5, 2026, when GPT-5.3 Codex and Opus 4.6 released simultaneously (00257):

- "Something clicked. Not like a light switch — more like the moment you realize the water has been rising around you and is now at your chest."
- AI demonstrated something that "felt, for the first time, like judgment. Like taste."
- GPT-5.3 Codex was "instrumental in creating itself" — the first public acknowledgment of recursive self-improvement in production.

This moment crystallizes the gap between public perception and practitioner reality. "The gap between what I've been saying and what is actually happening has gotten far too big" (00257).

The "21 actions" thread (00270, 03900) — 2M+ views — captures how acceleration beliefs translate into behavior:
- **Investing**: Direct AI exposure, liquidity over retirement vehicles, avoid 30-40 year lock-ins
- **Career**: Build distribution not credentials, become AI-native, avoid long training programs expecting ROI
- **Lifestyle**: Stop planning with 30-40 year horizons, check off bucket lists now, buy land near family
- **Finance**: Pull forward cash flows, lock fixed-rate debt, perceive debt 10+ years out as "likely nonexistent"

This represents an emerging accelerationist lifestyle philosophy — rational behavior given AGI assumptions, reckless behavior if assumptions are wrong.

## Antipatterns & Lessons
- **Definition as weapon**: Using the absence of consensus AGI definition to dismiss capabilities that are clearly transformative (01365, 00023).
- **Anchoring to outdated experience**: "I tried ChatGPT in 2023 and it wasn't that good" — the most dangerous form of dismissal because it was once true (00257).
- **Confusing timeline uncertainty with timeline distance**: Uncertainty about whether AGI arrives in 2027 or 2029 does not mean it might not arrive until 2040. On an accelerationist reading, the distribution is narrow and near — though this remains contested outside frontier lab circles.
- **Treating predictions as independent**: When Amodei, Hassabis, Legg, Musk, Kurzweil, and Epoch AI converge, that is not six separate predictions — it is a convergent signal from people with the most information.
- **Ignoring the METR exponential**: Individual predictions can be wrong; a trend line that has held for years across multiple model generations is structural evidence.
- **Accelerationist lifestyle risk**: Restructuring your life around AGI assumptions (abandoning retirement savings, taking on debt) is rational only if the timeline holds. If AGI arrives in 2035 instead of 2027, the financial advice in 00270 is catastrophic.
